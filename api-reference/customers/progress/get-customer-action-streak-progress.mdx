---
title: "Customer Stamps Progress"
description: "Retrieve the progress of a specific customer within a particular Stamps campaign in Gameball."
openapi: 'GET /api/v4.0/integrations/customers/{customerId}/stamps/{challengeId}'
languages: ["curl", "javascript", "python", "csharp"]
---

This API retrieves the progress of a specific customer within a particular Stamps campaign in Gameball. It returns how many steps the customer has completed, how many times they've earned the campaign reward, and whether they are eligible to continue.

<Info>
**Security**: Requires **apikey** and **secretkey** headers.
</Info>
