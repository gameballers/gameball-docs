---
title: "Customer Action Streak Progress"
description: "Retrieve the progress of a specific customer within a particular Action Streak challenge in Gameball."
openapi: 'GET /api/v4.0/integrations/customers/{customerId}/action-streak/{challengeId}'
languages: ["curl", "javascript", "python", "csharp"]
---

This API retrieves the progress of a specific customer within a particular Action Streak challenge in Gameball. It returns how many steps the customer has completed, how many times they've earned the challenge reward, and whether they are eligible to continue.

<Warning>
**Security**: Requires **apikey** and **secretkey** headers.
</Warning>
