# Welcome to Gameball 

**Gameball** isn't just a loyalty platform—it's a dynamic engine that powers customer engagement and retention. Designed to elevate your brand's relationship with your audience, Gameball offers a suite of gamified features that captivate and retain customers throughout their journey.

<Tip>
Instead of spending countless hours building loyalty and gamification features from scratch, Gameball provides a ready-made widget, allowing you to focus on customizing and refining the experience.
</Tip>

## Powerful REST API

**Gameball** [REST API](/api-reference/introduction) v4.0 follows RESTful principles with resource-oriented URLs, form-encoded request bodies, and JSON-encoded responses. Whether you're integrating through our REST API or using one of our SDKs, Gameball makes it simple to add loyalty and engagement features to your app or website.

<Note>
You can also customize your customers' rewards and points experience directly through the Gameball Dashboard. For more details about our features, visit the Gameball Help Center.
</Note>
