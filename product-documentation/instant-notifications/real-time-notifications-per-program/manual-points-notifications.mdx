Available for all Gameball customers except for free packages

When you add or deduct points from a customer, you will need to instantaneously inform your customer how many points were added or removed and what their current points balance is. Follow the steps below to activate real-time notifications for adding or deducting points.

## How to Create Real-Time Notifications for When Points are Added or Deducted

You can access the notifications' global settings in the **Gameball Admin Dashboard**.

- From the left bar, Click **Notifications.**
- Choose between **In-App, Emails, & SMS.**
- Scroll to **Add/Deduct Points,** and select between **Add Points** and **Deduct Points.**

<Frame>
![Add/Deduct points navigation](/images/product-docs/instant-notifications/real-time-notifications-per-program/add-deduct-points-navigation.png)
</Frame>

Start writing the message itself. Construct a heading and a body for the notification.

## Script Updates for Adding/Deducting Points

You can add the transaction notifications script for all notifications through this page:

### 1. Add Points Message

Send real-time transaction notifications for any points manually added. Inform your customer how many points they have received, why they have received these points, and their current points balance.

### 2. Deduct Points Message

Send a real-time notification to your Customers upon manually deducting points from their account. If the points are deducted for a specific reason, make sure to inform them of how many points have been taken back and what their current points balance is.

### Script Variables for Add/Deduct Points Notifications

To send more personalized, more relevant, and clear notifications, add variables in between your messages by clicking the **+ icon**.

<Frame>
![Script variables](/images/product-docs/instant-notifications/real-time-notifications-per-program/script-variables-add-deduct.png)
</Frame>

You can use variables in the scripts like the ones below:

- **Customer Variables:** First Name, Display Name, Customer's Email, Referral Link, Mobile Referral Link, Points Earned, Tier-up Progress, Points, Points Before, Tier Up total progress.
- **Transaction Variables:** Equivalent value of total points, Equivalent value of earned points, Currency, Points Name, Referral Link, Mobile Referral Link, Paid Amount, Merchant Name, Branch Name, Return Days, Available Points, Pending Points.

### Preview and Change Notification Icon

Before activating the notification, you might need to take a look at what the notification will look like for customers. You can easily do so by clicking **Preview** on the top right of the edit page.

<Frame>
![Preview notification](/images/product-docs/instant-notifications/real-time-notifications-per-program/preview-notification-add-deduct.png)
</Frame>

When clicking on preview, you will be able to see what the notification looks like for customers. You can edit the notification icon and upload one by choosing **Upload Icon**, then click **Upload Icon**.

<Frame>
![Upload icon dialog](/images/product-docs/instant-notifications/real-time-notifications-per-program/upload-icon-dialog-add-deduct.png)
</Frame>

<Frame>
![Notification icon preview](/images/product-docs/instant-notifications/real-time-notifications-per-program/notification-icon-preview-add-deduct.png)
</Frame>

Click **Update** from the bottom right.

### Status Update for Add/Deduct Points Notifications

You can enable or disable the notification through the **Active Toggle**.

<Frame>
![Active toggle](/images/product-docs/instant-notifications/real-time-notifications-per-program/active-toggle-add-deduct.png)
</Frame>

<Note>
Do not forget to click on "**Save**" before you exit.
</Note>
