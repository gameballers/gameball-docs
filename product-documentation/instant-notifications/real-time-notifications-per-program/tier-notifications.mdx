---
title: "How Do I Send Real-Time Notifications on Tiers?"
description: "Notify your customers upon tiering up using Gameball's real-time notifications."
---

Available for all Gameball customers except for free packages

When a customer meets the requirements needed to join a higher tier, it is important to inform them that they are now on that new level so they can check the benefits they can now enjoy and engage more. Follow the steps below to activate real-time notifications for tiering up.

## How to Create Real-Time Notifications for When a New Tier is Reached

You can access the notifications' global settings in the **Gameball Admin Dashboard**.

- From the left bar, Click **Notifications.**
- Choose between **In-App, Emails, & SMS.**
- Scroll to **Tiers,** and select between **Tier Achieved.**

<Frame>
![Tiers navigation](/images/product-docs/instant-notifications/real-time-notifications-per-program/tiers-navigation.png)
</Frame>

Start writing the message itself. Construct a heading and a body for the notification. In case you are creating an email notification, write a clear subject line and body. [Learn how to Write an email notification](/product-documentation/instant-notifications/email-notifications/sending-achievement-emails).

## Script Updates for Tiers Notifications

You can add the notification script for all notifications through this page:

### Achieved Tier

Send a real-time notification to your customers upon leveling up to a higher tier. Let them know the name of the new tier they are on, the tier entry reward if there is any, and ask them to check the new tier's benefits.

## Script Variables for Add/Deduct Points Notifications

To send more personalized, more relevant, and clear notifications, add variables in between your messages by clicking the **+ icon**.

<Frame>
![Script variables](/images/product-docs/instant-notifications/real-time-notifications-per-program/script-variables-tier.png)
</Frame>

You can use variables in the scripts like the ones below:

- **Customer Variables:** First Name, Display Name, Customer's Email, Referral Link, Mobile Referral Link, Tier-up Progress, Points, Points Before, Tier Up total progress, Total Friends Referred.
- **Tier Variables:** Equivalent value of total points, Equivalent value of earned points, Currency, Points Name, Achieved tier, Previous Tier, Next Tier, Tier rewarded points, Referral Link, Mobile Referral Link, Tier Entry Reward, Tier Lifetime Reward.

### Preview and Change Notification Icon

Before activating the notification, you might need to take a look at what the notification will look like for customers. You can easily do so by clicking **Preview** on the top right of the edit page.

<Frame>
![Preview notification](/images/product-docs/instant-notifications/real-time-notifications-per-program/preview-notification-tier.png)
</Frame>

When clicking on preview, you will be able to see what the notification looks like for customers. You can edit the notification icon and upload one by choosing **Upload Icon**, then click **Upload Icon**.

<Frame>
![Upload icon dialog](/images/product-docs/instant-notifications/real-time-notifications-per-program/upload-icon-dialog-tier.png)
</Frame>

<Frame>
![Notification icon preview](/images/product-docs/instant-notifications/real-time-notifications-per-program/notification-icon-preview-tier.png)
</Frame>

Click **Update** from the bottom right.

### Status Update for Add/Deduct Points Notifications

You can enable or disable the notification through the **Active Toggle**.

<Frame>
![Active toggle](/images/product-docs/instant-notifications/real-time-notifications-per-program/active-toggle-tier.png)
</Frame>

<Note>
Do not forget to click on "**Save**" before you exit.
</Note>
