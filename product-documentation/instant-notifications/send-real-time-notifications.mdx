Through Gameball, you can control sending real-time transaction notifications to your customers for all programs. You can notify your customers of different events such as new sign-ups, cashback rewards, successful referrals, reaching a new tier, etc.

There are three forms of notifications you can set up:

- In-App Notification
- Email Notification
- SMS Notification (Contact Sales for Activation)

## What events can you send real-time notifications for?

- **Sign-ups:** when a new customer signs up.
- **Cashback:** when granting cashback rewards or when it is canceled.
- **Referral:** when a customer successfully refers a friend or receives a reward for it.
- **Adding and Deducting Points:** After you have manually added or deducted points.
- **Reward Campaigns:** when a customer unlocks or completes a requirement for a badge or a reward or just to motivate them to complete it.
- **Tiers:** when a customer moves up from one tier to another.
- **Rewards:** for transactional events such as redeeming points, points refund, points expiry, or coupon expiry.
