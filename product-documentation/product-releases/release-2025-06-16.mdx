    **How to Set It Up:**

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Select your campaign template">
        Choose **Scratch & Win**, **Slot Machine**, or **Spin the Wheel** campaign template.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Set audience to Anonymous">
        Set your Audience to **Anonymous**, then click **Customize Design**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Navigate to Lead Form tab">
        Go to the **Lead Form** tab and scroll down to **Terms and Conditions**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Add your hyperlink">
        Follow the format to create a hyperlink: `{Link text[[https://www.example.com](https://www.example.com)]}`
      </Step>
    </Steps>

## Core & Platform

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="New Saudi Riyal Symbol Support in Arabic Widget View" icon="coins">
    **Platform:** All | **Plan:** All | **Channel:** Web, Mobile

    We've introduced support for the **Saudi Riyal currency symbol (﷼)** in Gameball's widget, specifically when the widget is displayed in **Arabic** and **Saudi Riyal** is the selected currency.

    **Localized Experience:** Ensures your customers in Saudi Arabia see the correct and culturally familiar currency representation when engaging with your loyalty widget.

    **How to Enable:**

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Go to General Settings">
        Navigate to **Settings > Admin Settings > General > Currency** and click **Edit**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select Saudi Riyal">
        Select **Saudi Riyal** as your currency.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Enable symbol display">
        Check the box: **"Display currency as a symbol."**
      </Step>
      <Step title="Set widget language">
        Ensure your widget is set to Arabic.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/product-docs/product-releases/saudi-riyal-symbol-setup.png" alt="Saudi Riyal symbol setup" />
    </Frame>

    The **﷼ symbol** will appear live in your widget immediately after saving.

  <Accordion title="Option to Hide Trophy Icon in Widget" icon="eye-slash">
    **Platform:** All | **Plan:** All | **Channel:** Web, Mobile

    You can now hide the trophy icon and default status text in the widget when Level and Referral cards are disabled. This helps keep the widget clean and aligned with brand design when only the Points Balance and Earn/Redeem cards are active.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/product-docs/product-releases/hide-trophy-icon-widget.png" alt="Hide trophy icon in widget" />
    </Frame>

    Navigate to **Widget > General > Visibility Tab**. The option will be shown only in case the Tier and Referral cards are set to hidden.
