We've introduced a new Play & Win section in the Gameball widget to make games easier to find, easier to understand, and more engaging for customers. Instead of appearing alongside regular earning actions, all game-based campaigns now live in one dedicated place.

---

## What Is the Play & Win Section?

**Play & Win** is a new widget section that displays all active game campaigns in one place. It helps customers quickly discover games and clearly separates games from standard earning actions. [Learn about the Gameball Widget](/product-documentation/customer-widget/introduction-to-customer-widget).

The section appears automatically when at least one game is active and disappears when no games are available.

## Supported Game Types

All existing and upcoming game campaigns are supported, including:

<Info>
Spin & Win, Slot Machine, Scratch & Win, Quiz, Puzzle, Match Cards, Shooter, Tap the Target, Stamps, and more. Each campaign appears as a **separate card**, even if multiple campaigns use the same game type.
</Info>

## What Existing Gameball Customers Need to Do

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Nothing changes immediately" icon="circle-check">
    Your existing games will continue to appear under the **Earn** section. No action is required right away.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="When you edit an existing game campaign" icon="pen-to-square">
    You'll be asked to add or update the game's banner through the customization section.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/product-docs/product-releases/game-banner-customization.png" alt="Game banner customization" />
    </Frame>

    - This step is required to support the new Play & Win experience.
    - Once completed, the game will automatically move from **Earn** to **Play & Win**.
    - You can then control the visibility of this section of the widget by deciding to show or hide it. Note that hiding it will mean campaigns do not exist for your customer as they can't access it. [Learn how to control the visibility of each section/tab in the widget](/product-documentation/customer-widget/start-configuring-profile/customizing-tabs-visibility-and-order-in-gameball-widget-settings).

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/product-docs/product-releases/widget-section-visibility-control.png" alt="Widget section visibility control" />
    </Frame>

    - When you have multiple game campaigns customized, you will be able to control how they appear in the section as well as their order from the **play & win options** tab under the General widget section in the dashboard. [Learn how to control the order and card layout of the play & win section in the widget](/product-documentation/customer-widget/start-configuring-profile/customizing-tabs-visibility-and-order-in-gameball-widget-settings).

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/product-docs/product-releases/play-win-options-tab.png" alt="Play & Win options tab" />
    </Frame>

    This allows you to transition to the new layout when you're ready, without unexpected changes.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## What New Gameball Customers (or New Campaigns) Need to Do

For new accounts or newly created game campaigns:

- Adding a **banner** is required during setup.
- Games will appear **only** in the **Play & Win** section.
- Games will no longer appear under **Earn**.

<Tip>
This ensures a clean and consistent widget structure from the start.
</Tip>

## Customizing How Games Appear

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to the campaign">
    From your dashboard, go to the desired campaign, click **Edit**, then **Customize Design**.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/product-docs/product-releases/game-campaign-customize-design.png" alt="Game campaign customize design" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the Banner tab">
    Stay on the **Banner** tab. You can customize each game's icon, short description, and banner image.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/product-docs/product-releases/game-banner-customization-options.png" alt="Game banner customization options" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and preview">
    All changes are reflected in the widget automatically and support multiple languages, based on your widget's language settings.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Key Things to Know

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Section visibility rules" icon="eye">
    The Play & Win section only appears when at least one game is active. Inactive or expired games are hidden automatically.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Countdown timer" icon="clock">
    A countdown timer appears when a game is close to ending (within 12 hours), creating urgency for customers.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
