  <Accordion title="SMS Reminder for Expiring Points" icon="message-sms">
    **Platform:** All Platforms | **Plan:** Plans supporting SMS | **Channel:** Mobile

    We've introduced a new option within SMS notifications that allows you to **automatically notify customers 15 days before their points expire**, helping boost engagement and reduce unused points and rewards. [Learn how to create an SMS campaign](/product-documentation/communication-campaigns/create-and-configure-sms-campaigns).

    <CardGroup cols={3}>
      <Card title="Customer Retention" icon="user-check">
        Give customers a gentle nudge to come back and redeem their points before they lose them.
      </Card>
      <Card title="Increased Redemption Rates" icon="chart-line">
        Prompt action from customers who may otherwise forget about expiring points.
      </Card>
      <Card title="Frictionless Communication" icon="bolt">
        Seamlessly integrated within Gameball's communication system — no manual scheduling needed.
      </Card>
    </CardGroup>

    **How It Works:**

    - A **pre-expiry SMS notification** is triggered **15 days before the customer's points expire**.
    - You can **enable or disable** this feature directly from the SMS notifications settings page.
    - Message content follows your configured SMS template and branding.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Sort the Redemption Options Order in the Widget" icon="arrow-up-arrow-down">
    **Platform:** All Platforms | **Plan:** All | **Channel:** All

    With our new **Redemption Sorting Controls**, you can now fully manage the display order of your redemption options inside the widget, highlighting key rewards and simplifying navigation for your customers. [Learn how to control widget component visibility.](/product-documentation/customer-widget/start-configuring-profile/customizing-tabs-visibility-and-order-in-gameball-widget-settings)

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/product-docs/product-releases/redemption-sorting-controls.png" alt="Redemption sorting controls" />
    </Frame>

    **Three Sorting Modes:**

    | Mode | Description |
    |------|-------------|
    | Auto by Creation Date | Displayed chronologically by setup date |
    | Auto by Points Required | Sorted from lowest to highest cost |
    | Manual Sorting | Drag and drop redemption items to reorder them exactly how you like |

    <Info>
      **Pinned Dynamic Rewards:** "USD 0.01 per point" or similar dynamic options are always shown at the top, regardless of sorting method. **Real-Time Preview** lets you instantly visualize how sorting changes will look inside your live widget.
    </Info>
  </Accordion>

## Other Improvements

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Exclude Specific Customers Across All Campaign Types and Modules" icon="user-minus">
    We've rolled out the ability to **exclude specific customers** from **all campaign types**, including **Reward Campaigns** and **Automation Campaigns**, as well as across **every Gameball module** that supports audience selection.

    <CardGroup cols={3}>
      <Card title="Greater Control" icon="sliders">
        Fine-tune your targeting strategy by removing certain customers from specific promotions or messages.
      </Card>
      <Card title="Avoid Overexposure" icon="shield">
        Prevent power customers or test accounts from being included in active campaigns.
      </Card>
      <Card title="Enhance Personalization" icon="user-gear">
        Exclude irrelevant audiences for better-performing, more focused customer experiences.
      </Card>
    </CardGroup>

    **Example:** Exclude VIP customers from entry-level campaigns or testing groups from live promotions.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Bulk Untag Customers" icon="tags">
    **Platform:** All Platforms | **Plan:** All | **Channel:** All

    Gameball customers can now **bulk remove customer tags** directly from the dashboard or by uploading the Update/Migrate CSV file, making tag management just as flexible and efficient as bulk tagging.

    | Feature | Details |
    |---------|---------|
    | Dashboard Access | Use the `Add/Remove Tags` option under the "More" menu in the Customers section |
    | Upload Option | Support for `Tags(Remove)` direct upload in the `Update/Migrate` CSV template |
    | Smart Tag Logic | Already removed tags are ignored to avoid redundant actions |
    | Real-Time Visibility | See applied changes instantly across the customer table and segmentation filters |
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="External Terms & Conditions Link in FAQ" icon="file-contract">
    **Platform:** All Platforms | **Plan:** All | **Channel:** All

    You can now add external redirection links to Terms & Conditions inside the "What is Points" and "What is Score" sections in the widget FAQ.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/product-docs/product-releases/faq-terms-conditions-link.png" alt="FAQ terms and conditions link" />
    </Frame>

    **How It Works:**

    - Use this format: `{Link Text[[https://yourdomain.com/terms](https://yourdomain.com/terms)]}` when editing the FAQ text.
    - Available only inside "What is Points?" and "What is Score?"

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/product-docs/product-releases/faq-terms-conditions-dashboard.png" alt="FAQ terms and conditions in dashboard" />
    </Frame>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Actual Order Date/Time Now Available in Exported Sheets" icon="calendar">
    **Platform:** All Platforms | **Plan:** All | **Channel:** All

    Gameball now includes the original order date and time (submitted via the Order API) in the exported transactions sheet from the Activities tab.

    - A new column has been added to show the **actual order date/time** sent.
    - This is separate from the existing "received at" timestamp (when Gameball received the order).
    - Available in all newly exported files.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
