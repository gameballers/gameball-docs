### Milestone: New Reward Campaign

| Platform | Plan |
|----------|------|
| Non-Platform | Available |
| Shopify or Salla | Contact Support |
| All | Add-on |

The **Milestone Campaign** is a rule-based campaign that accumulates a customer's _eligible_ spend or item count across orders during a defined period (e.g., **SAR 500 per month**). Milestone campaign helps increase **AOV** and **repeat purchase frequency**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/product-releases/milestone-campaign.png" alt="Milestone campaign" />
</Frame>

**Use Cases:**

1. **Spending Milestones** — Reward based on total spend within a period. _Example:_ "Spend **$1000** and get a **$100** gift card."
2. **Collection/Tag/SKU/Branch-Specific Milestones** — Only spending on a chosen collection/category/SKU counts. _Example:_ "Spend **$500** on **Electronics** and get **$50** off your next order."
3. **Items-Purchased Milestones (count)** — Reward by quantity of items, regardless of price. _Example:_ "Buy **10** items and get **20%** off next order."

Read this [Help Center Article](/product-documentation/rewards-campaigns/faqs/stamps-milestone-mission-campaigns) to learn more.

### 1. Dynamic Value for Fixed Coupon Rewards

| Platform | Plan | Channel |
|----------|------|---------|
| All | All | All |

We've enhanced the **Fixed Coupon Rewards** by introducing a **Dynamic Value option**. Instead of being limited to a static reward (e.g., $10 off), you can now configure fixed coupons to dynamically calculate their value based on a percentage of a chosen variable (e.g., **15% of Value X**). [Learn more about how to set up each Reward type.](/product-documentation/rewards-campaigns/faqs/understanding-rewards-types)

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/product-releases/dynamic-fixed-coupon-rewards.png" alt="Dynamic fixed coupon rewards" />
</Frame>

**Use Case**

Instead of always giving $10 off, a merchant can now set the coupon to equal **15% of the customer's cart total or a specific metadata value**, making offers more personalized and impactful.

**Key Highlights**

- **Dynamic Option Added to Fixed Coupons** — Select whether a fixed coupon has a **static fixed value** (e.g., $10) or a **dynamic value** (percentage of a chosen metric).
- **Flexible Promotions** — Easily adapt offers to cart totals, order values, or metadata-driven actions.
- **Seamless Setup** — No changes required in workflow; just configure the value type when setting up your fixed coupon rewards.

### 2. Coupons Start & End Date

| Platform | Plan | Channel |
|----------|------|---------|
| All | All | All |

We've added **Start and End Date options** for coupons, giving you full control over when your coupons are active. This ensures promotions run only during intended time frames and automatically expire when no longer valid. [Learn more about creating a new coupon](/product-documentation/coupons/creating-coupon-manually).

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/product-releases/coupons-start-end-date.png" alt="Coupons start and end date" />
</Frame>

**Use Cases**

- Launch a coupon that starts automatically on **Black Friday** and expires at the end of **Cyber Monday**.
- Set seasonal coupons (e.g., valid only for the December holiday shopping season).
- Prevent outdated coupons from being used after a promotion ends.

**Key Highlights**

- **Start Date** — Merchants can define when the coupon becomes valid.
- **End Date** — Coupons automatically expire on the set date.
- **Automation** — No need to manually activate or deactivate coupons.
- **Consistency** — Works across all coupon types (fixed, dynamic, third-party, etc.).

<Note>Coupon start and end dates are only available when the campaign is **scheduled**.</Note>

### 3. Copy Campaigns Across Workspaces

| Platform | Plan |
|----------|------|
| Non-Platform | Available for clients with active Workspaces |

Introducing the ability to **copy reward campaigns across workspaces**, making it easier than ever to replicate successful campaigns without starting from scratch. [Learn about workspaces](/product-documentation/admin-settings/creating-multiple-workspaces).

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/product-releases/copy-campaigns-workspaces.png" alt="Copy campaigns across workspaces" />
</Frame>

**Use Cases**

- Roll out the same loyalty campaign across different regions.
- Standardize campaign structures while leaving room for local customization.
- Ensure campaign logic and rewards remain aligned across workspaces.

**Key Highlights**

1. **Copy Campaign Action** — A new **"Copy to Workspace"** button is now available in campaign actions. Copy a campaign to one or multiple workspaces at once.
2. **Event Handling** — If the original campaign is tied to an event not available in the target workspace, the event will automatically be created and the copied campaign will link to it.
3. **Language Compatibility** — If the target workspace has languages not available in the original campaign, those language fields will be left empty for the client to update later.
4. **Campaign Status & Audience Defaults** — Copied campaigns will always be **inactive by default** (to avoid unintended launches). The audience will always be set to **All Audience** by default.
5. **Data Migration** — The following data will be copied: Campaign Name, Description, Icon, Rewards configuration (points, coupons, etc.).

### 5. "Is Referred": New Customer Attribute

We've added a new **Boolean customer attribute** to help you identify whether a customer was referred by another customer or acquired through other channels. [Learn more](/product-documentation/understanding-your-customers/customer-profile/customer-profile-attributes)

**API Enhancements**

- A new field `isReferred` is now included in the `customerAttributes` object for:
  - `GET /player/{playerUniqueId}`
  - `GET /player/details/{playerUniqueId}`
- **Definition**: `true` → Customer was referred by another customer. `false` → Customer was not referred (acquired through other channels).

**Dashboard Enhancements**

- **Customer Profile** — New attribute **Is Referred** added under **Customer Attributes**.
- Available as a **filter option** in: Campaigns, Segments, Automations.

This allows you to target only customers who were not referred, or create campaigns and segments tailored to referred vs. non-referred users.

### 6. Full Control Over Internal Gameball Events

| Platform | Plan | Channel |
|----------|------|---------|
| All | All | Dashboard → Integrations / Event Settings |

You now have the ability to **enable or disable the internal events** that Gameball automatically fires (such as `place_order`, `view_product`, `order_refunded`, `add_to_cart`, etc.). [Learn more about creating and sending events to Gameball](/product-documentation/admin-settings/create-and-send-events).

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/product-releases/control-internal-events.png" alt="Control internal Gameball events" />
</Frame>

**Why This Matters**

This gives you **complete control** over the events you track and process, allowing you to eliminate unnecessary or duplicate events, customize your tracking to fit your business logic, and decide which events should (or shouldn't) be fired to your system.

**Key Details**

- A **dedicated settings page** now lists all auto-fired Gameball events with toggles (Enable/Disable).
- Changes apply **in real time**; once an event is disabled, it stops being fired immediately.
- When disabled, the event will no longer be sent to your system or used in internal Gameball flows (unless you manually fire it).
- **Permissions:** Only Admins, Workspace Owners, or Workspace Admins can control event toggles. If custom roles are enabled, this action is assignable.

### 7. Custom Expiry for Points Multiplier Campaigns

| Platform | Plan |
|----------|------|
| All | All |

You can now **set a configurable expiry period** for points earned through **point multiplier campaigns**. This gives you full control over **when bonus points are activated** (respecting your return window) and **when they expire**, helping reduce fraud risk and liability. [Read about points multiplier campaigns](/product-documentation/rewards-campaigns/start-from-scratch/create-point-multiplier-reward-campaign).

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/product-releases/custom-expiry-multiplier.png" alt="Custom expiry for points multiplier" />
</Frame>

Previously, points earned from multiplier campaigns were **activated immediately** without considering your return/refund window, exposing merchants to fraud risk and inflated liability from early activation of bonus points.

**How It Works**

<Steps>
<Step title="Set Expiry in Campaign Setup">
  While creating/editing a **Point Multiplier Campaign**, you'll see a new **Expiry (days)** field. Accepts only numeric values (Min: 1 day, Max: 365 days). Required field.
</Step>

<Step title="Activation Logic">
  Points (base + multiplier) are **activated only after the program-level return window ends**, ensuring no points can be used during refund eligibility.
</Step>

<Step title="Expiration Logic">
  Once activated, points expire after the **custom expiry period you set** (e.g., 30 days).
</Step>

<Step title="Validation & Errors">
  If expiry is missing or invalid, you'll see an error message before saving.
</Step>
</Steps>

### 8. Automate "Points to Expire" Notifications

| Platform | Plan | Channel |
|----------|------|---------|
| All | All | All |

You can now configure automations using a **"Points to Expire" trigger**. This gives you full control over **when and how to notify customers about their expiring points**, helping you drive engagement while reducing liability. [Learn how to create an Automation Campaign](/product-documentation/automation-campaigns/how-to-create-an-automation-campaign).

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/product-releases/points-to-expire-automation.png" alt="Points to expire automation" />
</Frame>

Before today, Gameball sent fixed **points expiration notifications** by default with limited flexibility. With this update, you get **custom timing** for expiry reminders, **multi-channel communication** (Email, SMS, Push, Webhook), and **full control** — no expiry notifications will be sent unless you set up automations.

**How It Works**

<Steps>
<Step title="New Trigger: Points to Expire">
  Available in the **Automation Builder**. Detects when a customer's points are **approaching expiration** and lets you define a **custom timeframe** (e.g., 30, 15, 7, or X days before expiry).
</Step>

<Step title="Available Actions">
  Choose one or more actions to notify customers: Send Email, Send SMS, Send Push Notification, Send Webhook.
</Step>

<Step title="Flexible Automation Flows">
  Create multiple automations for different timeframes (e.g., a reminder 30 days before and a final reminder 7 days before). Add **conditions** like "only trigger if expiring points ≥ 50."
</Step>

<Step title="Message Customization">
  Use dynamic placeholders in templates: `#{{customer_name}}`, `#{{expiring_points}}`, `#{{expiry_date}}`. Tailor your messages per channel.
</Step>

<Step title="Execution Logs & Reporting">
  Gameball logs each automation execution. You'll see which customers were targeted and what actions were performed.
</Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
The old **Points Expiry Notifications** section has been removed. No expiry notifications will be sent **by default** unless you configure this automation. A ready-to-use **premade template** is available to mimic the previous expiry notification behavior for quick setup.
</Warning>

### 2. MoEngage Integration: Country & Currency Support

| Platform | Plan | Channel |
|----------|------|---------|
| All | All | All |

Gameball now includes **Country** and **Currency** information in customer data sent to **MoEngage**, giving you more flexibility to **segment customers, filter transactions, and personalize campaigns** based on location and currency context. [Read about Gameball on Moengage](/product-documentation/apps-integrations/moengage-integration).

**What's New**

- **Customer Events** — For all **Purchase/Order** and **Refund** events sent to MoEngage, the payload now includes **country** (the country defined for the workspace) and **currency** (the workspace currency code, e.g., USD, EGP, EUR).
- **Customer Attributes** — New attributes **Country** and **Currency** are added when syncing customers with MoEngage.
- **Detection Logic** — Country uses a new workspace-level attribute; Currency uses the existing workspace currency setting, always sent as a **3-letter currency code**.
- **Condition** — These fields are included **only if** the workspace has both country and currency configured **and** MoEngage integration is enabled.

With this enhancement, MoEngage users can segment customers by geography, personalize campaigns with currency-aware messaging, and filter or trigger flows based on workspace-level country & currency data.

### 3. Workspace Information Synced to MoEngage

| Platform | Plan | Channel |
|----------|------|---------|
| All (with Multi-Workspace enabled) | All plans supporting MoEngage | MoEngage Integration |

We've enhanced our **MoEngage integration** to include **workspace context** in both customer events and attributes, giving you greater flexibility in segmentation and personalization. [Read about Gameball on Moengage](/product-documentation/apps-integrations/moengage-integration).

For clients managing multiple workspaces, this update makes it possible to **segment and target customers in MoEngage based on the specific workspace** where their activity or data originates.

**Key Highlights**

1. **Customer Events Update** — All customer events sent to MoEngage now include an additional property: **`gameball_workspace`** (a string containing the workspace name or ID), present across **all events** fired to MoEngage.
2. **Customer Attributes Update** — A new **Gameball Workspace** customer attribute stores the associated workspace, allowing MoEngage users to filter and segment customers directly by workspace.
3. **Condition** — These new fields are **only included if the client is using a workspace-enabled setup** (multi-workspace account).
