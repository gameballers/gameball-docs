Our customer support team is always there for you. If you have any inquiries but do not know how to directly contact any of our customer support agents, check the following steps. There are three channels where you can easily reach any of our agents and simply send your inquiry.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
<Card title="Gameball Dashboard" icon="gauge" href="https://app.gameball.co">
  Contact support directly while working inside your Gameball dashboard.
</Card>

<Card title="Gameball Website" icon="globe" href="https://gameball.co">
  Reach support from the main Gameball website.
</Card>

<Card title="Help Center" icon="book-open" href="https://help.gameball.co">
  Find answers or contact an agent through the Gameball help center.
</Card>

<Card title="Previous Messages" icon="clock-rotate-left" href="https://app.gameball.co">
  Review your past conversations with the support team.
</Card>
</CardGroup>

## How to Contact Customer Support on Any Gameball Channel

<Steps>
<Step title="Find the Support Icon">
  If you are at any Gameball channel, you will always find a blue circle with a message-like icon. Click on the **circle**.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/product-docs/guides-strategies-faqs/contact-support-icon.png" alt="Contact Support Icon" />
  </Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Choose Your Action">
  Then choose to either **chat with us** or check **previous messages**.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/product-docs/guides-strategies-faqs/contact-support-menu.png" alt="Contact Support Menu" />
  </Frame>
</Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>You can always find answers through our help center by visiting: help.gameball.co</Tip>
