Our customer support team is always there for you. If, however, you have any inquiries but do not know how to directly contact any of our customer support agents, check the following steps. There are three channels where you can easily reach any of our agents and simply send your inquiry.

- On Gameball dashboard
- On Gameball Website
- In our help center

## How to contact customer support on any of Gameball channels

If you are at any Gameball channel, you will always find a blue circle with a message-like icon. Click on the **circle.**

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/guides-strategies-faqs/contact-support-icon.png" alt="Contact Support Icon" />
</Frame>

Then choose to either **chat with us** or check **previous messages**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/guides-strategies-faqs/contact-support-menu.png" alt="Contact Support Menu" />
</Frame>

You can always find answers through our help center by visiting: help.gameball.co
