If you are running both a Shopify online store and an offline retail store using Shopify POS, you can also let your in-store customers get rewards through Gameball.

---

## Getting Started with Gameball on Shopify POS

Once you install Gameball on your Shopify account, it will be automatically available to both your Shopify online store and POS channels.

To enable Gameball on Shopify POS, use the same method as publishing Gameball on your online store. Learn [how to publish the Gameball experience and widget on your Shopify store](/product-documentation/guides-strategies-faqs/supportive-guides/publish-gameball-experience).

<Note>You must create an account for your customers using their email to get rewards from Gameball — the same as the online experience.</Note>

## Programs Available for In-Store Customers

<AccordionGroup>

<Accordion title="Tiers" icon="layer-group">
Even for your in-store customers, you can tier them across your Gameball tiers based on the amount spent, points, number of completed orders, number of referrals, or score.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Earn Points" icon="star">
Your in-store customers will be treated the same as your online customers, so that they will get all configured programs:

For ways to earn that depend on events, on POS, we will have only one Shopify event working, which is **"Place Order."** Learn more about [how to use events on Shopify](/product-documentation/guides-strategies-faqs/shopify-guides/using-events).

## How Customers Redeem Points During a Purchase

You can create two types of point redemption for POS customers:

| Redemption Type | Description |
|---|---|
| **POS All Points Redemption** | Let customers redeem all their points at once during transactions at physical stores using Shopify POS. |
| **POS Fixed Amount Coupons** | Let customers exchange their points for a fixed amount coupon, e.g. USD 20 coupon. |
| **POS Percentage Coupons** | Let customers exchange their points for a percentage discount, e.g. 20% discount coupon. |

To enable any of the previous POS coupon creations, create a new redemption rule for POS. Follow the steps under [this article to enable this option](/product-documentation/programs/loyalty-points-redeem/launch-and-configure-your-redeem-pointing-system).
