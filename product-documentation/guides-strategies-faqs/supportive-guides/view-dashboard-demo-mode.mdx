| Platform | Availability |
|----------|-------------|
| Self-Serve (Non-Platform) | Available |
| Shopify / Salla | Not Available |

For **Self-Serve** customers only. If you are new to Gameball dashboard experience and you still have not filled your program with data about your customers, you can now view the dashboard on **Demo Mode** just to get a glimpse of how the dashboard would look like filled with data and program configurations.

The data you will find once you get to the dashboard demo mode is dummy; this means they do not represent actual information of real customers or of launched programs or campaigns.

---

## How to View the Dashboard on Demo Mode

<Steps>
<Step title="Open Demo Account">
  It takes only one click to jump from your real dashboard to a demo mode. Just click on **Settings > Demo Account**.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/product-docs/guides-strategies-faqs/demo-account-menu.png" alt="Demo Account Menu" />
  </Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Explore the Demo">
  As soon as you click on **Demo Account**, a new tab will open, and you will be able to see some dummy data all over it. Feel free to surf all tabs and programs. You can also try some of the programs' dummy configurations on your real dashboard.

  <Note>The **Demo Account** button is only found on **non-platform** accounts.</Note>
</Step>
</Steps>

---

## How Do I Get Back to My Real Dashboard?

As mentioned, when clicking on **Demo Account**, a new tab will open, which means your real dashboard is going to be there still.

---

## How Do I See the Widget on Demo View?

<Steps>
<Step title="Click Preview Widget">
  You will find a bar that invites you to check a demo view of the widget. Click **Preview Widget**.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/product-docs/guides-strategies-faqs/preview-widget-bar.png" alt="Preview Widget Bar" />
  </Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Explore the Demo Widget">
  A new tab will open previewing a Gameball demo store along with a demo reward widget. Check it out and live a loyalty program customer experience.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/product-docs/guides-strategies-faqs/demo-widget-preview.png" alt="Demo Widget Preview" />
  </Frame>
</Step>
</Steps>
