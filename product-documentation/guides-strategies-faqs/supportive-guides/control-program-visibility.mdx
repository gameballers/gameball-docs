title: "How Do I Control Gameball Program Visibility on My Platform?"
description: "Learn about the three different stages that help you control the presence of the Gameball widget on your platform."
---

There are different ways to control your customer's interaction with the widget, starting from entirely eliminating the widget to only hiding the button. Here are the three different stages through which you can control the Gameball widget.

---

## Stage 1: Publish and Unpublish the Gameball Loyalty Experience

By toggling the switch at the top right of any page on the **Gameball Admin Dashboard**, you will be able to activate or deactivate the Gameball loyalty experience.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/guides-strategies-faqs/publish-toggle.png" alt="Publish Toggle" />
</Frame>

Once you toggle to unpublish, your customers will no longer find the Gameball widget on your platform. Any actions like purchases done by your customers during the time Gameball is inactive will still reflect on Gameball; however, your customers will not be rewarded for their activity.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/guides-strategies-faqs/unpublish-confirmation.png" alt="Unpublish Confirmation" />
</Frame>

<Note>When the Gameball program is switched to unpublished, any customer event will still reflect on their profile on the dashboard; however, they will **appear dimmed** as these actions will have no consequence.</Note>

Once you switch it back on, the widget will appear again on your platform, and your customers will start getting rewarded again.

### What Happens When You Deactivate the Program?

| Effect | Description |
|---|---|
| Program visibility | Your loyalty program will **not** be visible to customers |
| Reward earning | Your customers will **not** earn rewards |
| Notifications | Your customers will **not** receive any messages, notifications, or emails |
| Customer actions | Your customers' actions will still be added to Gameball |
| Existing coupons | Your customers will be able to use coupons they had already claimed before disabling the program |

<Warning>
- Gameball will still be receiving any actions when the toggle is deactivated. **However**, no changes will take place as a result of these customer actions.
- Publishing Gameball means that your customers will be able to see the Gameball widget (unless the widget is hidden), and they will start getting rewards based on the designed loyalty experience.
</Warning>

---

## Stage 2: Hiding and Unhiding the Gameball Widget

You can completely hide Gameball's widget without deactivating the loyalty program, which means your customers will still be rewarded for their interaction; however, they will not be able to track their progress or find anything related to the configured loyalty program.

<Steps>
<Step title="Navigate to Widget Visibility Settings">
  Go to **Gameball Admin Dashboard**, click **Widget Settings > General: Visibility**.
</Step>

<Step title="Set Widget Visibility">
  Under **Widget**, choose whether to display or hide the Gameball widget by checking **Show** or **Hide** under Customer and guest, then click **Save**.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/product-docs/guides-strategies-faqs/widget-visibility-settings.png" alt="Widget Visibility Settings" />
  </Frame>
</Step>
</Steps>

A disclaimer should guide you to understand whether the customers can see the widget or not:

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/guides-strategies-faqs/widget-visibility-disclaimer.png" alt="Widget Visibility Disclaimer" />
</Frame>

## Stage 3: Hiding and Unhiding the Gameball Widget Button

Lastly, you can choose between hiding or displaying the Gameball Launch button on your platform. Suppose there is no space for the Gameball launch button on your platform, or none of Gameball's available buttons match your brand theme. In that case, you can simply hide it and let other inline buttons or any other interactive elements open the Gameball widget. Read more on [Enabling Opening Gameball Widget by Using Deeplinks](/product-documentation/customer-widget/player-profile-tabs/widget-deep-links).

<Steps>
<Step title="Navigate to Launcher Settings">
  Go to **Gameball Admin Dashboard**, click **Widget Settings > Launcher > Shape tab**.
</Step>

<Step title="Hide the Launcher Button">
  Under **Launcher**, choose the option to hide the Gameball launch button, then click **Save**.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/product-docs/guides-strategies-faqs/launcher-hide-button.png" alt="Hide Launcher Button" />
  </Frame>
</Step>
</Steps>

Now, the Gameball button will no longer appear on your platform, so make sure to use our [Deep Links](/product-documentation/customer-widget/player-profile-tabs/widget-deep-links) if you still want your customers to access the widget from a different place on your platform.
