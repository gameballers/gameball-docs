---
title: "How Do I Install Gameball on Your Shopify Store?"
description: "Learn how to launch the Gameball experience to your customers on Shopify."
---

Creating and launching the Gameball experience on your Shopify store requires only a few steps. Follow the steps below to install Gameball:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find and Install Gameball on Shopify Marketplace">
    - Go to [https://apps.shopify.com/](https://apps.shopify.com/)
    - Type "Gameball" in the search bar
    - Choose **Gameball: Loyalty Points Games**

    <Frame caption="Search for Gameball on Shopify App Store">
      ![Search for Gameball](/images/product-docs/integrate-gameball/shopify-search-gameball.png)
    </Frame>

    - Click **Add app**

    <Frame caption="Add Gameball app">
      ![Add app](/images/product-docs/integrate-gameball/shopify-add-app.png)
    </Frame>

    - Insert your Shopify store URL
    - Click **Log in** and sign in to your Shopify account

    <Frame caption="Shopify login">
      ![Shopify login](/images/product-docs/integrate-gameball/shopify-login.png)
    </Frame>

    - You will be redirected to the Shopify Store Admin page, click **Install app**

    <Frame caption="Install app on Shopify Admin">
      ![Install app](/images/product-docs/integrate-gameball/shopify-install-app.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Complete the onboarding process and configure an account on the Gameball Admin Dashboard">
    Once you click Install app on Shopify, you will be redirected to Gameball Admin to create an account and access Gameball Dashboard.

    - Fill in your information, then click **Create Account**

    <Frame caption="Create Account on Gameball">
      ![Create Account](/images/product-docs/integrate-gameball/shopify-create-account.png)
    </Frame>

    - Choose the dashboard language

    <Frame caption="Choose dashboard language">
      ![Choose language](/images/product-docs/integrate-gameball/shopify-choose-language.png)
    </Frame>

    - Select a suitable plan

    <Frame caption="Select a plan">
      ![Select plan](/images/product-docs/integrate-gameball/shopify-select-plan.png)
    </Frame>

    When you select a plan, you might get redirected to the Shopify Admin page to complete your billing information.

    - Complete the billing details and click **Approve Subscription**

    <Frame caption="Approve subscription on Shopify">
      ![Approve subscription](/images/product-docs/integrate-gameball/shopify-approve-subscription.png)
    </Frame>

    - Complete the onboarding wizard on the Gameball admin dashboard
  </Step>

  <Step title="Publish Gameball Customer Widget on Your Store">
    When you have completed the onboarding steps, you need to publish the configured Gameball experience by switching the **activation toggle** on the top right of any page.

    <Frame caption="Activation toggle to publish Gameball widget">
      ![Activation toggle](/images/product-docs/integrate-gameball/shopify-activation-toggle.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>
