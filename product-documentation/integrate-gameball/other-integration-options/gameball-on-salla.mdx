Gameball is now available on Salla's marketplace! This integration offers Salla's customers a seamless way to enhance their online stores with powerful gamification and loyalty features.

Let's dive into how you can integrate Gameball into your Salla store and unlock a world of engagement and retention possibilities.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install Gameball from the Marketplace">
    The integration process begins with a simple click. Head over to Salla's marketplace and locate the Gameball plugin. With just a few clicks, you can install Gameball into your Salla store, opening the door to a host of engagement tools designed to delight your customers.

    <Frame caption="Gameball on Salla marketplace">
      ![Gameball on Salla marketplace](/images/product-docs/integrate-gameball/salla-marketplace-install.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Account Creation on Gameball">
    Once Gameball is installed, an account will be automatically created for you on the Gameball platform. This account serves as your gateway to crafting personalized loyalty programs, setting up engaging challenges, and harnessing the power of gamification to drive customer loyalty and retention.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Receive Credentials via Email">
    After the account creation process, keep an eye on your inbox. You'll receive an email from Gameball containing your login credentials. These credentials are your key to unlocking Gameball's robust dashboard and accessing a suite of tools designed to elevate your Salla store's performance.

    <Frame caption="Gameball credentials email">
      ![Gameball credentials email](/images/product-docs/integrate-gameball/salla-credentials-email.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Log in and Complete the Wizard">
    Once you have your credentials, log in to the Gameball dashboard to begin customizing. The intuitive wizard will walk you through setup, enabling you to tailor Gameball's features to your store's needs and goals. From loyalty programs to engaging campaigns, it's simple to create an immersive shopping experience that boosts customer retention with just a few clicks.

    <Frame caption="Gameball onboarding wizard">
      ![Gameball onboarding wizard](/images/product-docs/integrate-gameball/salla-onboarding-wizard.gif)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

Integrating Gameball into your Salla store is a game-changer for e-commerce enthusiasts looking to elevate their online presence. With a seamless installation process and intuitive setup wizard, you can quickly unlock the power of gamification and loyalty to drive engagement, retention, and revenue.
