Collecting emails from visitors who haven't signed up yet is essential as it opens more channels of communication between you and potential customers and it helps you take initiative that might convert visitors into customers.

<Frame>
  ![Pop-up campaign preview](/images/product-docs/rewards-campaigns/templates/acquisition-conversion/popup.png)
</Frame>

Nevertheless, if you send newsletters, you might want to get approval from your customers as well as your visitors to receive these emails. There is no better way to collect emails and/or get newsletter approvals than the **Pop-up Campaigns** Gameball has created for you.

<Warning>
- This feature is intended for **Web-based businesses only**. It should not be enabled for non-web platforms (e.g., mobile apps).
- Make the necessary adjustments to the Activation, Details, Rewards, and Goal sections. [Learn more about setting up a Reward Campaign.](/product-documentation/rewards-campaigns/start-from-scratch/setup-reward-campaign)
</Warning>

<Frame>
  ![Pop-up campaign demo](/images/product-docs/rewards-campaigns/templates/acquisition-conversion/popup-demo.gif)
</Frame>

Here are the pop-up campaigns ready-to-use templates currently available.

## Subscribe to Newsletter (for Shopify Users)

Encourage customers to give you their emails as approval for their subscription to your newsletter. Once the customer writes down their emails, they will automatically be marked as subscribed on Shopify. You can customize a reward that is convenient for this approval. For example, you can reward them with 10% off for doing so.

### How to Set Up

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose Your Audience">
    Select the audience for the campaign from the following options:
    - All customers (This includes Anonymous and Registered visitors)
    - Anonymous customers only
    - Registered customers only
    - Specific customer segments

    <Frame>
      ![Choose audience](/images/product-docs/rewards-campaigns/templates/acquisition-conversion/choose-audience.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Trigger Delay">
    Set a delay to your **Trigger** if needed. Select when the pop-up should appear:
    - Immediately (Default)
    - After a delay: you can specify the number of seconds for the delay (e.g., 5 seconds).

    <Frame>
      ![Trigger delay settings](/images/product-docs/rewards-campaigns/templates/acquisition-conversion/trigger-delay.png)
    </Frame>

    <Note>
    - **Display frequency:** The pop-up will be shown **once per session.** This means that the customer will not be seeing this more than once unless they completely close their browser and open it in a new session.
    - If a customer is eligible for more than one newsletter subscription campaign, they will only be rewarded the first time they give their email address.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize Your Pop-up">
    Create the content, look and feel of the pop-up so that it is clear and helpful. Choose **Customize Design**.

    <Frame>
      ![Customize design button](/images/product-docs/rewards-campaigns/templates/acquisition-conversion/customize-design.png)
    </Frame>

    Customize both the **Main pop-up** and the **Success pop-up**.

    <Frame>
      ![Main pop-up customization](/images/product-docs/rewards-campaigns/templates/acquisition-conversion/main-popup.png)
    </Frame>

    <Frame>
      ![Success pop-up customization](/images/product-docs/rewards-campaigns/templates/acquisition-conversion/success-popup.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Campaign Insights">
    Choose **Insights** to see how the campaign is doing in terms of performance.

    <Frame>
      ![Campaign insights](/images/product-docs/rewards-campaigns/templates/acquisition-conversion/insights.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Unlock x% of Your First Order

Similarly, incentivize customers especially visitors who have not signed up yet to give their emails by rewarding them with a discount when doing so.

<Warning>
- This feature is intended for **Web-based businesses only**. It should not be enabled for non-web platforms (e.g., mobile apps).
- Make the necessary adjustments to the Activation, Details, Rewards, and Goal sections. [Learn more about setting up a Reward Campaign.](/product-documentation/rewards-campaigns/start-from-scratch/setup-reward-campaign)
</Warning>

### How to Set Up

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose Your Audience">
    Select the audience for the campaign from the following options:
    - All customers (This includes Anonymous and Registered visitors)
    - Anonymous customers only
    - Registered customers only
    - Specific customer segments

    <Frame>
      ![Choose audience](/images/product-docs/rewards-campaigns/templates/acquisition-conversion/choose-audience-2.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Trigger Delay">
    Set a delay to your **Trigger** if needed. Select when the pop-up should appear:
    - Immediately (Default)
    - After a delay: you can specify the number of seconds for the delay (e.g., 5 seconds).

    <Frame>
      ![Trigger delay settings](/images/product-docs/rewards-campaigns/templates/acquisition-conversion/trigger-delay-2.png)
    </Frame>

    <Note>
    - **Display frequency:** The pop-up will be shown **once per session.** This means that the customer will not see this more than once unless they completely close their browser and open it in a new session.
    - If a customer is eligible for more than one newsletter subscription campaign, they will only be rewarded the first time they give their email address.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Campaign Settings">
    Choose Your **Audience** and edit your **trigger** by adding the **Collection ID** to the **Place Order** event that is already set up.

    <Frame>
      ![Collection ID trigger](/images/product-docs/rewards-campaigns/templates/acquisition-conversion/collection-id-trigger.png)
    </Frame>

    Make the necessary adjustments to the Activation, Details, Rewards, and Goal sections. [Learn more about setting up a Reward Campaign.](/product-documentation/rewards-campaigns/start-from-scratch/setup-reward-campaign)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Customize Your Pop-up">
    Create the content, look, and feel of the pop-up so that it is clear and helpful. Choose **Customize Design**.

    <Frame>
      ![Customize design](/images/product-docs/rewards-campaigns/templates/acquisition-conversion/customize-design-2.png)
    </Frame>

    Customize both the **Main pop-up** and the **Success pop-up**.

    <Frame>
      ![Main pop-up customization](/images/product-docs/rewards-campaigns/templates/acquisition-conversion/main-popup-2.png)
    </Frame>

    <Frame>
      ![Success pop-up customization](/images/product-docs/rewards-campaigns/templates/acquisition-conversion/success-popup-2.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review Campaign Insights">
    Choose **Insights** to see how the campaign is doing in terms of performance.

    <Frame>
      ![Campaign insights](/images/product-docs/rewards-campaigns/templates/acquisition-conversion/insights-2.png)
    </Frame>

    The analytics section provides an overview of the campaign's performance such as:

    - **Distinct customers Count:** Unique players achieved this campaign.
    - **Total Impressions:** Total Number of pop-up views.
    - **Total Subscriptions**
    - **Total Rewarded Coupons**
    - **Total Rewarded Points**
    - **Conversion Rate:** The percentage of users who achieved the campaign (_Calculated as: Total Subscription / Total Pop-up Views._)
    - **Dismissal Rate:** The percentage of users who dismissed the pop-up (_Calculated as: Total Dismissals / Total Pop-up Views._)
    - **Impressions vs Subscriptions overtime Report**
  </Step>
</Steps>
