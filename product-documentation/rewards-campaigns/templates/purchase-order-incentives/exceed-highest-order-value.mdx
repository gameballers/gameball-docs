Motivate customers to increase their order value by offering rewards for larger purchases. Each time the customer exceeds their highest order value, they will earn a reward that you set up. This tactic can help boost average order value and maximize revenue per transaction.

<Frame>
  ![Exceed highest order value campaign widget](/images/product-docs/rewards-campaigns/templates/purchase-order-incentives/exceed-highest-order-widget.png)
</Frame>

---

## How to Set Up

<Steps>
<Step title="Open Gameball">
  Log in to your Gameball Admin Dashboard.
</Step>

<Step title="Add a New Reward Campaign">
  Click **Add Reward Campaign**.
</Step>

<Step title="Select the Template">
  Select the **Exceed your highest order value** template.
</Step>

<Step title="Edit the Campaign Setup">
  Configure your campaign's audience, rewards, and other settings.
</Step>

<Step title="Set Live">
  Click **Set Live** to activate your campaign.
</Step>
</Steps>

---

## Campaign Trigger

This campaign rewards customers for placing orders:

1. Each time a customer places an order that exceeds their previous highest order value, they earn a reward.
2. You can set a minimum order value to activate the campaign (e.g., the order must exceed a specific amount to qualify, such as $100).

<Frame>
  ![Exceed highest order value trigger setup](/images/product-docs/rewards-campaigns/templates/purchase-order-incentives/exceed-highest-order-trigger.png)
</Frame>

**Repeatability:**

Customers can earn this reward unlimited times, as long as each new order exceeds their previous highest order value. This setting cannot be customized.

This template uses the standard reward campaign setup. See the [Generic Setup Guide](/product-documentation/rewards-campaigns/start-from-scratch/setup-reward-campaign) for details.

---

## How It Works for Customers

<Steps>
<Step title="Customer Places an Order">
  Customer places an order with an order value higher than their previous highest purchase.
</Step>

<Step title="Customer Receives the Reward">
  Customer gets rewarded immediately (points, discount, badge — based on your setup).
</Step>

<Step title="Reward Notification">
  The customer is notified via the widget or email, depending on your configuration. [Learn more](/product-documentation/rewards-campaigns/start-from-scratch/setup-reward-campaign)
</Step>
</Steps>

---

## Important Notes

<Note>
**Earning Rules**

1. This campaign only triggers on placed order events.
2. Customers can earn the reward unlimited times, as long as each new order exceeds their previous highest value.
</Note>
