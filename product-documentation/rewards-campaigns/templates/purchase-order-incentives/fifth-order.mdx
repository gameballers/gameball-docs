Rewarding customers after completing five orders is a powerful way to acknowledge loyalty and encourage continued engagement. Reaching five purchases is a clear indicator of strong satisfaction with your brand, and a reward at this stage helps build emotional connection and long-term retention. It's a great opportunity to reinforce habits and turn regular customers into true brand advocates.

## How to Set Up

<Steps>
<Step title="Open Gameball">
  Log in to your Gameball Admin Dashboard.
</Step>

<Step title="Add a New Reward Campaign">
  Click **Add Reward Campaign**.
</Step>

<Step title="Select the Template">
  Select the **Fifth Order** Template.
</Step>

<Step title="Edit the Campaign Setup">
  Configure your campaign's audience, rewards, and other settings.
</Step>

<Step title="Set Live">
  Click **Set Live** to activate your campaign.
</Step>
</Steps>

---

## Campaign Trigger

The campaign trigger fires when the customer places **5 orders** on your platform:

- **Action:** Place Order
- **Times:** 5

<Frame>
  ![Fifth order trigger setup](/images/product-docs/rewards-campaigns/templates/purchase-order-incentives/fifth-order-trigger.png)
</Frame>

This template uses the standard reward campaign setup. See the [Generic Setup Guide](/product-documentation/rewards-campaigns/start-from-scratch/setup-reward-campaign) for details.

---

## How It Works for Customers

<Steps>
<Step title="Customer Places 5 Orders">
  A customer places **5 orders** after the campaign is activated.
</Step>

<Step title="Reward is Triggered">
  Gameball automatically triggers the reward.
</Step>

<Step title="Customer Receives the Reward">
  The customer receives the reward immediately (points, discount, badge — based on your setup).
</Step>

<Step title="Reward Notification">
  The customer is notified via the widget or email, depending on your configuration. [Learn more](/product-documentation/rewards-campaigns/start-from-scratch/setup-reward-campaign)
</Step>
</Steps>

---

## Important Notes

<Note>
**New Orders Only**

Only customers who place five new orders **after** the campaign is activated will be rewarded.
</Note>

<Note>
**No Retro Rewarding**

Orders placed before the campaign is activated will **not** be counted. The customer must place **five new orders after activation** to receive the reward.
</Note>
