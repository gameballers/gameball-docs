Encourage product discovery and increase average order value by rewarding customers who shop across multiple collections. The **_Buy from 3 Different Collections_** campaign is perfect for cross-selling and promoting variety in customer purchases. By targeting collection diversity, you can guide customers to explore more of what your store offers.

## How to Set Up

<Steps>
<Step title="Open Gameball">
  Log in to your Gameball Admin Dashboard.
</Step>

<Step title="Add a New Reward Campaign">
  Click **Add Reward Campaign**.
</Step>

<Step title="Select the Template">
  Select the **Buy from 3 Different Collections** template.
</Step>

<Step title="Edit the Campaign Setup">
  Configure your campaign's audience, rewards, and other settings.
</Step>

<Step title="Set Live">
  Click **Set Live** to activate your campaign.
</Step>
</Steps>

---

## Campaign Trigger

The campaign trigger is configured as follows:

1. **Action:** Place Order
2. **Times:** 1 _(Number of orders required with this condition)_
3. **Rule:**
   - **Field:** Collection ID
   - **Operator:** Different values
   - **Value:** 3 _(Number of different collections required in a single order)_

<Frame>
  ![Buy from 3 different collections trigger setup](/images/product-docs/rewards-campaigns/templates/purchase-order-incentives/buy-from-3-collections-trigger.png)

## How It Works for Customers

<Steps>
<Step title="Customer Places an Order">
  A customer places an order that includes products from **three different collections**.
</Step>

<Step title="Reward is Triggered">
  Gameball automatically triggers the reward.
</Step>

<Step title="Customer Receives the Reward">
  The customer receives the reward immediately (points, discount, badge — based on your setup).
</Step>

<Step title="Reward Notification">
  The customer is notified via the widget or email, depending on your configuration. [Learn more](/product-documentation/rewards-campaigns/start-from-scratch/setup-reward-campaign)
</Step>
</Steps>

---

## Important Notes

<Note>
**Collection Diversity Required**

The order must include products from **three different collections** to trigger the reward.
</Note>
