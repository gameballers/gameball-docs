Encourage higher-order values and repeat purchases with the **_Buy Over $50 and Get $5 on the Next Order_** campaign. This setup is perfect for motivating customers to spend more now in exchange for a clear reward they can redeem later, helping you boost both short-term revenue and long-term retention.

## How to Set Up

<Steps>
<Step title="Open Gameball">
  Log in to your Gameball Admin Dashboard.
</Step>

<Step title="Add a New Reward Campaign">
  Click **Add Reward Campaign**.
</Step>

<Step title="Select the Template">
  Select the **Buy Over $50 and Get $5** template.
</Step>

<Step title="Edit the Campaign Setup">
  Configure your campaign's audience, rewards, and other settings.
</Step>

<Step title="Set Live">
  Click **Set Live** to activate your campaign.
</Step>
</Steps>

---

## Campaign Trigger

The campaign trigger is configured as follows:

1. **Action:** Place Order
2. **Times:** 1 _(Number of orders required with this condition)_
3. **Rule:**
   - **Field:** Total Price
   - **Operator:** >= (Greater than or equal)
   - **Value:** 50

<Frame>
  ![Buy over $50 trigger setup](/images/product-docs/rewards-campaigns/templates/purchase-order-incentives/buy-over-50-get-5-trigger.png)
</Frame>

**Reward:**

- The customer gets a **$5** coupon.
- The reward value can be adjusted from the campaign.

<Frame>
  ![Buy over $50 reward setup](/images/product-docs/rewards-campaigns/templates/purchase-order-incentives/buy-over-50-get-5-reward.png)
</Frame>

This template uses the standard reward campaign setup. See the [Generic Setup Guide](/product-documentation/rewards-campaigns/start-from-scratch/setup-reward-campaign) for details.

## How It Works for Customers

<Steps>
<Step title="Customer Places an Order">
  A customer places an order worth **$50 or more**.
</Step>

<Step title="Reward is Triggered">
  Gameball automatically triggers the reward.
</Step>

<Step title="Customer Receives the Reward">
  The customer receives a **$5 discount**, which can be applied to their **next order**.
</Step>

<Step title="Reward Notification">
  The customer is notified via the widget or email, depending on your configuration. [Learn more](/product-documentation/rewards-campaigns/start-from-scratch/setup-reward-campaign)
</Step>
</Steps>

---

## Important Notes

<Note>
**Minimum Order Value Required**

Only orders with a **total price of $50 or more** will trigger the reward.
</Note>
