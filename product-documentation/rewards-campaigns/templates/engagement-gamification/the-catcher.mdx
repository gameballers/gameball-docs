An interactive gamification tool that boosts engagement and brand awareness. Customers try to catch positive icons and miss bad ones to unlock rewards, driving interaction, visibility, and new revenue opportunities.

**Use Cases:**

1. **Boost Activity:** Encourage daily or weekly logins with regular gameplay and rewards to drive retention.
2. **Brand-Sponsored Challenges:** Brands can sponsor games, driving engagement and creating new revenue streams for Gameball customers.

## How to Set Up

<Steps>
<Step title="Open Gameball">
  Log in to your Gameball Admin Dashboard.
</Step>

<Step title="Add a New Reward Campaign">
  Click **Add Reward Campaign**.
</Step>

<Step title="Select the Template">
  Select the **Catcher** Template.
</Step>

<Step title="Edit the Campaign Setup">
  Configure your campaign's audience, rewards, repeatability, and platform visibility.
</Step>

<Step title="Set Live">
  Click **Set Live** to activate your campaign.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Campaign Trigger

Customers can access the Catcher game through two entry points:

- **On Widget:** Customers can access the catcher game through the **Badges** section in the widget. Tapping the badge redirects them to a screen where they can start playing.
- **Deep Link:** Use the deep link to trigger the catcher campaign from a custom banner or button on your website or app. [Learn more](/product-documentation/rewards-campaigns/start-from-scratch/setup-reward-campaign).

**Audience Options:**

- **Registered Customers:** Customers who have an account on your platform (signed up).
- **Anonymous Customers:** Visitors browsing anonymously without providing any contact information or identifiable details (not signed up).

  <Note>
  Visitors can open the game, submit basic information through the lead form, play, and receive rewards, matching the standalone game URL experience. Widget deep links that open a specific game work for guests as well — the widget opens directly into the game screen and allows participation without login.
  </Note>

- **Specific Customers:** You can choose a specific selection of customers to participate in this campaign by filtering using segments, tags, or customer attributes.

**Repeatability:**

By default, customers can play **once per day**. A new opportunity to replay begins at 00:00 AM based on the chosen time zone. All Games campaigns support only the Limit per day/week/etc. [Learn more](/product-documentation/rewards-campaigns/start-from-scratch/setup-reward-campaign).

## Game Rules

Go to **Customize Design -> Game** to set up the rules.

**Dropping Items**

1. The game includes **items to catch** and **items to avoid** by default, and you can add up to 5 items each.
2. Customers must catch enough items and reach the required score **before the timer runs out**.

<Frame>
  ![The catcher dropping items](/images/product-docs/rewards-campaigns/templates/engagement-gamification/the-catcher-items.png)
</Frame>

**Minimum Items to Collect & Time**

- Specify the minimum amount of items that should be caught to win before time runs out.
- Specify the duration (in seconds) during which the customer is allowed to play the game.

<Frame>
  ![The catcher minimum items and time](/images/product-docs/rewards-campaigns/templates/engagement-gamification/the-catcher-time.png)
</Frame>

**Lives**

- To add difficulty, you can **limit the number of lives**. If customers lose more lives than specified by collecting bad items, they lose.

<Frame>
  ![The catcher lives setting](/images/product-docs/rewards-campaigns/templates/engagement-gamification/the-catcher-lives.png)
</Frame>

## How It Works for Customers

<Steps>
<Step title="Move the Basket">
  Customers move the cursor to slide the basket left and right. Random items are dropping.
</Step>

<Step title="Catch and Avoid">
  The customer tries to catch good items and must avoid bad ones to win.
</Step>

<Step title="Game Ends">
  The game ends when the time runs out or when the customer loses the maximum number of lives.
</Step>

<Step title="Reward Notification">
  The customer is notified via the widget or email, depending on your configuration, when they earn a reward. [Learn more](/product-documentation/rewards-campaigns/start-from-scratch/setup-reward-campaign).
</Step>
</Steps>

## Customization

You can fully customize each stage of the campaign through dedicated tabs representing the different screens in the flow:

- **Banner**
- **Intro / Main Screen**
- **Success Screen**
- **No Luck Screen**
- **Game General Rule Customizations:**
  - **Time Duration:** Set how long each game lasts (e.g., 10s, 20s, 60s).
  - **Mistakes/Lives:** Choose how many lives a customer has (e.g., 1, 3, or unlimited).
  - **Minimum Number of Items to Collect.**
  - **Tooltips:** Added help text explaining how time and lives impact gameplay.

For each screen, you can tailor the following elements:

- **Branding:** Adjust background colors, text colors, and upload custom artwork.
- **Content:** Edit the headlines, descriptions, and call-to-action messages to match your tone and messaging.

## Important Notes

<Note>
**Customer Eligibility**

Games in the widget update dynamically based on customer eligibility, ensuring customers only see games they can still participate in.

**Games are hidden when customers become ineligible.**

If a customer no longer belongs to the game's target segment, the game is automatically removed from the widget and won't reappear unless eligibility is restored.
</Note>
