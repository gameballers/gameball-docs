Rewarding your customers is a powerful way to drive engagement, increase loyalty, and boost sales. In this article, we'll introduce the different types of Reward Campaigns and highlight our ready-made templates designed to help you engage, acquire, retain, personalize, grow, and celebrate special occasions with your customers.

## Start a Campaign from Scratch

To start creating a campaign from scratch, pick one of the following campaign types:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Action-Based Reward" icon="bolt" href="/product-documentation/rewards-campaigns/start-from-scratch/create-action-based-reward-campaign">
    Reward customers based on specific actions they take, such as making a purchase, referring a friend, or completing a survey.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Time-Based Rewards" icon="calendar" href="/product-documentation/rewards-campaigns/start-from-scratch/create-time-based-reward-campaign">
    Reward customers on certain dates or occasions, such as birthdays, anniversaries, or holidays, to create a sense of exclusivity and appreciation.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Points Multiplier Rewards" icon="xmark" href="/product-documentation/rewards-campaigns/start-from-scratch/create-point-multiplier-reward-campaign">
    Multiply the points earned by customers when they make a purchase, previously known as Cashback Campaigns, to incentivize spending and drive repeat purchases.
  </Card>
  <Card title="[Shopify] Shopify Flow Rewards" icon="shopify" href="/product-documentation/rewards-campaigns/templates/engagement-gamification/gameball-on-shopify-flow">
    Create custom rewards triggered by specific actions on apps connected to Shopify Flow, letting you tailor rewards for unique actions like social engagement.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Ready-Made Reward Campaign Templates

Gameball offers a range of ready-made templates categorized based on your objectives. These templates can be **filtered by reward campaign types.**

Before diving into the available templates, you must recognise the different types of templates you can distinguish through the **Tags** and **Icons** on each card.

| Tag | Description |
|-----|-------------|
| Shopify Flow | Available only on Shopify Flow. |
| Pop-up | This campaign does not only appear on the widget, but could be displayed as a pop-up when your customers visit your platform. This guarantees higher participation number. |
| Hard | Usually found on Game related campaigns. Means it is relatively more difficult to play and win than the other games. |
| Crown icon | Available on under a specific plan. |

## How to Use Reward Campaigns

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your Gameball Dashboard">
    From the left side menu, navigate to the **Campaigns** section and click on **Rewards**.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/product-docs/rewards-campaigns/navigate-to-rewards.png" alt="Navigate to Rewards in the dashboard" />
    </Frame>

    You are now on the reward campaigns page, where you can see and manage data on previously created campaigns.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose a template or create from scratch">
    Choose the desired **template based on your objectives**, or choose to create a **new reward campaign from scratch**.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/product-docs/rewards-campaigns/create-reward-campaign.png" alt="Create a new reward campaign" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure and activate your campaign">
    Make the necessary adjustments and **activate** the reward campaign to start rewarding your customers and driving desired behaviors.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Manage Your Campaigns Efficiently

Gameball offers convenient shortcuts to manage your reward campaigns seamlessly. When you access your campaigns through the **Gameball Admin Dashboard,** you'll find a comprehensive **list of all your created campaigns** along with their details.

By **clicking on the points** beside each campaign, you'll unlock a range of options to streamline your campaign management process.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/rewards-campaigns/campaign-shortcuts-menu.png" alt="Campaign management shortcuts" />
</Frame>

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/rewards-campaigns/campaign-shortcuts-options.png" alt="Campaign shortcut options" />
</Frame>

| Shortcut | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Duplicate | Create an identical copy of the selected campaign. This is useful when you need to replicate a campaign with minor adjustments. |
| Activate / Deactivate | Toggle the active status of the campaign. Easily enable or disable a campaign to control its visibility and functionality. |
| Edit | Make adjustments to the settings and details of the selected campaign. Access the campaign's inner page to modify its configuration as needed. |
| Copy to Workspace | Copy that same campaign to a different workspace. |
| Campaign URL | Copy campaign URL. |
| Delete | Permanently remove the selected campaign. Exercise caution when using this option, as deleted campaigns cannot be recovered. |

By utilizing these shortcuts, you can efficiently manage your reward campaigns, saving time and ensuring that your customer engagement strategies remain dynamic and effective.
