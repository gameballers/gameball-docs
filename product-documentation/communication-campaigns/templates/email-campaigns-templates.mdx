[Learn more about creating and configuring email campaigns](/product-documentation/communication-campaigns/create-and-configure-email-campaigns).

## How to Use the Templates

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to Dashboard">
    Log in to your **Gameball Dashboard** and navigate to the **Campaigns section**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Communication Campaigns">
    Choose **Communication Campaigns**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a template">
    Choose the desired template from the list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview and adjust">
    Send a **test email** to preview the template and make any necessary adjustments.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set live">
    Once satisfied, set the campaign **live**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Check this article for a [detailed guide on how to create a new email](/product-documentation/communication-campaigns/create-and-configure-email-campaigns) or make edits on a template.

## Benefits of Using Ready-Made Templates

- **Time-Saving:** Eliminate the need to design email campaigns from scratch, saving valuable time and resources.
- **Consistency:** Maintain brand consistency across all communication channels with professionally designed templates.
- **Effectiveness:** Leverage proven email templates optimized for user engagement and conversion.
