Effective communication with your audience is essential for driving engagement and achieving your business goals. With Gameball's Web Messages Communication Campaign Templates, you can effortlessly create targeted messages to boost customer interaction and drive desired actions. [Learn more about creating and configuring web message campaigns](/product-documentation/communication-campaigns/create-and-configure-web-message-campaigns).

In this guide, we'll introduce two pre-designed templates tailored to enhance point redemption and increase referral link shares.

## Available Web Messages Templates

### Increase Points Redemption

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/communication-campaigns/template-web-increase-redemption.png" alt="Increase points redemption web message template" />
</Frame>

#### Criteria

- **Trigger:** On Page visit - URL contains "/cart" (**Update your URL and add the cart URL**)
- **Audience:** Specific Customer
- **Customer Attribute:** Points > 100

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/communication-campaigns/template-web-increase-referrals.png" alt="Increase referral link shares web message template" />

- **Trigger:** Action-based
- **Event:** "Place an order"
- **Audience:** Registered Customers

## How to Use the Templates

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to Dashboard">
    Log in to your **Gameball Dashboard** and navigate to the **Campaigns section**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Communication Campaigns">
    Choose **Communication Campaigns**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a template">
    Choose the desired template from the list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview and adjust">
    Check the **preview**, and make any necessary adjustments.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set live">
    Once satisfied, set the campaign **live**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Check this article for a [detailed guide on how to create new web messages](/product-documentation/communication-campaigns/create-and-configure-web-message-campaigns) or make edits on a template.

## Benefits of Using Web Message Templates

- **Personalization:** Tailor messages to specific audience segments based on their behavior or attributes.
- **Automation:** Set triggers and targeting criteria to automate message delivery and maximize efficiency.
- **Engagement:** Capture users' attention with timely and relevant messages, encouraging interaction and action.
- **Conversion:** Drive desired actions, such as points redemption or referral link shares, to increase customer loyalty and sales.
