## Setup Guide

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-113.png" />
</Frame>

<Steps>
<Step title="Log in to the Gameball Dashboard">
  Log in to the **Gameball Dashboard**.
</Step>

<Step title="Navigate to Rewards Campaigns Templates">
  Navigate to the **Rewards Campaigns Templates** page by clicking **Campaigns > Rewards > Create New Reward Campaign**.
</Step>

<Step title="Select the Shopify Flows Template">
  Select the **Shopify Flows' Write a Review** Template under **Social Sharing & Reviews**.
</Step>

<Step title="Configure and Save the Campaign">
  Configure your campaign settings and click **Save**. Then copy the **Campaign ID**.
</Step>

<Step title="Create a Workflow in Shopify Flow">
  Open Shopify Flow and create a new workflow (as per the video above).
</Step>

<Step title="Set the Yotpo Trigger">
  Set **Yotpo** as your **trigger**.
</Step>

<Step title="Configure the Gameball Action">
  Choose Gameball's **Reward a Campaign** as the **action**. [Learn More](/product-documentation/apps-integrations/shopify-flow/introduction-to-shopify-flow)

  1. Add your customer **email** by clicking on **add variable** (skip the email if the trigger already passes the ID).
  2. Copy and paste the **Campaign ID** from the campaign details page on Gameball.
</Step>

<Step title="Activate the Workflow">
  Activate your workflow and track its impact on customer engagement.
</Step>
</Steps>
