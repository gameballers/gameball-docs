---
title: "How Do I Integrate Reviews.io Through Shopify Flow?"
description: "Reward your customers for their valuable feedback using Gameball's integration with Reviews.io via Shopify Flow."
---

With Gameball's integration with Reviews.io through Shopify Flow, you can automatically reward customers for leaving reviews. This integration helps boost customer engagement and loyalty by offering incentives for valuable feedback.

## Benefits

- **Increased Engagement** - More reviews lead to higher interaction with your brand.
- **Strengthened Loyalty** - Rewarding customers for their feedback builds stronger relationships.
- **Valuable Insights** - Gain more insights through increased review volume.

---

## Setup Guide

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-112.png" />
</Frame>

<Steps>
<Step title="Create a Reward Campaign in Gameball">
1. Log in to the **Gameball Dashboard**.
2. Navigate to **Campaigns > Rewards > Create Reward Campaign**.
3. Select the **Shopify Flows' Write a Review** template under **Social Sharing & Reviews**.
4. Configure your campaign settings and click **Save**.
5. Copy the **Campaign ID**.
</Step>

<Step title="Set Up the Shopify Flow Workflow">
1. Open Shopify Flow and create a new workflow.
2. Set **Reviews.io** as your **trigger**.
3. Choose Gameball's **Reward a Campaign** as the **action**. [Learn More](/product-documentation/apps-integrations/shopify-flow/introduction-to-shopify-flow)
   - Add your customer **email** by clicking **Add variable**.
   - Copy and paste the **Campaign ID** from the campaign details page on Gameball.
4. Activate your workflow and track its impact on customer engagement.
</Step>
</Steps>
