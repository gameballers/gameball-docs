If you are using Zapier on your platform for workflows or automation, use Gameball to engage and reward your customers with a few simple clicks.

---

## Why Use Gameball with Zapier?

Using Gameball with Zapier allows you to send events to Gameball in order to reward your customers for completing and performing certain actions on hundreds of other platforms — like subscribing to a newsletter, clicking on a link inside an email, and much more!

<Info>
Before you log in to your Zapier account and start connecting other apps to Gameball, make sure to create and configure these events through the Gameball dashboard. To learn more about recommended events and how to configure them, check this [article](/product-documentation/guides-strategies-faqs/supportive-guides/ecommerce-events).
</Info>

---

## How to Integrate Zapier on Gameball

For the sake of illustration, we will connect Judge.me to Gameball, where Judge.me sends an event to Gameball every time a new review is written so that Gameball can reward your customers for writing reviews.

<Steps>
<Step title="Log in to Zapier and Search for Apps">
  Log in to your **Zapier Dashboard**. Search for Judge.me and Gameball to connect them.

  <Note>
  Make sure you use **Gameball V1.1**.
  </Note>

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-38.png" />
  </Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Select the Trigger and Action">
  Select the **trigger New Review** on Judge.me and **Send Event** action on Gameball.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-39.png" />
  </Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Confirm the Connection">
  You have now successfully connected Judge.me with Gameball, and your customers will be rewarded according to your Gameball configuration.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-40.png" />
  </Frame>
</Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Enjoy connecting more apps to Gameball to reward your customers or to keep data synced.
</Tip>
