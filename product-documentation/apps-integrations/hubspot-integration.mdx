### Why should you integrate HubSpot with your Gameball account? And how can you make the best out of Gameball customer attributes?

This integration will allow you to target and segment your customers based on how loyal they are to your app. This integration will allow you to:

1.  Check Gameball Custom attributes on HubSpot.

2.  Â Trigger a workflow based on Gameball customer attributes.


## How to configure HubSpot on Gameball?

-   Go to **Integrations** on your **Gameball Admin Dashboard**


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-41.png" />
</Frame>

-   Click on **Connect** under **HubSpot**


-   Install HubSpot from the Shopify App Store

-   Get your API Key from your HubSpot account through **Settings > Integrations**

-   Copy this API token


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-42.png" />
</Frame>

-   Paste it on your Gameball Admin Dashboard account and click **Next**


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-43.png" />
</Frame>

-   Click **Add** so that this list of customer attributes will be automatically added to your HubSpot account.


**_Note:_** _Make sure you use these attributes with the notation provided on your Gameball Admin Dashboard, as described below._

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-44.png" />
</Frame>

-   Go to your HubSpot account and check the Custom attributes of a contact. Go to **Contacts > Select a specific contact (customer)**


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-45.png" />
</Frame>

-   Trigger a workflow based on a Gameball customer attribute. Go to **Automation > Workflows > Actions > Set Enrollment Trigger > Contact Properties**


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-46.png" />
</Frame>
