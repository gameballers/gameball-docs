Integrating HubSpot with Gameball allows you to target and segment your customers based on how loyal they are to your app.

## Why Integrate HubSpot with Gameball?

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Check Custom Attributes" icon="user-check">
    Check Gameball custom attributes directly on HubSpot contact profiles.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Trigger Workflows" icon="bolt">
    Trigger a workflow based on Gameball customer attributes to automate engagement.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## How to Configure HubSpot on Gameball

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Integrations">
    Go to **Integrations** on your **Gameball Admin Dashboard**.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-41.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect HubSpot">
    Click on **Connect** under **HubSpot**. Install HubSpot from the Shopify App Store.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Get Your API Key">
    Get your API Key from your HubSpot account through **Settings > Integrations**. Copy this API token.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-42.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Paste API Token into Gameball">
    Paste the API token into your Gameball Admin Dashboard account and click **Next**.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-43.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Customer Attributes">
    Click **Add** so that this list of customer attributes will be automatically added to your HubSpot account.

    <Note>
      Make sure you use these attributes with the notation provided on your Gameball Admin Dashboard, as described below.
    </Note>

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-44.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="View Custom Attributes in HubSpot">
    Go to your HubSpot account and check the Custom attributes of a contact. Navigate to **Contacts > Select a specific contact (customer)**.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-45.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Trigger Workflows Based on Gameball Attributes">
    Trigger a workflow based on a Gameball customer attribute. Go to **Automation > Workflows > Actions > Set Enrollment Trigger > Contact Properties**.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-46.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>
