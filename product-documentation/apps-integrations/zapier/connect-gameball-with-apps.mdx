---

Learn how to use Zapier to let Gameball reward your players after completing actions on other apps like subscribing, submitting a survey, reactivating an account, completing payments, etc.

<Info>
A Zap consists of a trigger and an action. Gameball is only working as an action. Read more on [Gameball on Zapier](/product-documentation/apps-integrations/zapier-integration).
</Info>

</Frame>

## How to Create a Zapier Connection

<Steps>
<Step title="Choose a Trigger and an Action App">
  Follow these steps to create a Zap between Gameball and any other app on Zapier:

  - Go to [zapier.com](http://zapier.com)
  - In the left box, search for any app you want to set as the [trigger app](/product-documentation/apps-integrations/zapier/connecting-gameball-to-zapier).

  In the upcoming steps, we will use **SurveyMonkey** as the trigger app to guide you on how to give players a reward upon survey form submission.

  <Note>
  In order to use this feature, you must be on a paid plan on SurveyMonkey.
  </Note>

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-128.png" />
  </Frame>

  - Search for **"Gameball"** in the right box.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-129.png" />
  </Frame>

  - Select a trigger, then choose **"Send Event"** to be the selected action Gameball will execute.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-130.png" />
  </Frame>

  - Click **Try it**.
</Step>

<Step title="Configure the Trigger on Zapier">
  On Zapier, a **trigger** is any event that you want to use to start a Zap. Read more on [triggers](https://zapier.com/help/create/basics/set-up-your-zap-trigger) on Zapier.

  In this use case, the trigger will be done through SurveyMonkey. Make sure that your players submit their email addresses in the survey. The email address submitted must match the one on your platform stored at Gameball.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-131.png" />
  </Frame>

  <AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="A. Choose App and Event" icon="wand-magic-sparkles">
    Choose SurveyMonkey app and select Trigger Event as **"New Response Notification"**.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-132.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="B. Choose Account" icon="user">
    In case you have different SurveyMonkey accounts, choose the one you are expecting campaigns through.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-133.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="C. Customize Response" icon="pen-to-square">
    Choose the name of the survey you're expecting the response to.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-134.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="D. Choose Collector" icon="inbox">
    Choose the collector you're expecting this response on.

    <Note>
    A collector on SurveyMonkey is a shorthand for describing the way responses are being collected on SurveyMonkey.
    </Note>

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-135.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="F. Test Trigger & Find Data" icon="flask">
    Click on **"Test trigger"** to make sure you will find a response and verify your trigger is working fine before customizing your Zap.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-136.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Accordion>
  </AccordionGroup>
</Step>

<Step title="Configure the Action">
  An **action** is a job your Zap does once your trigger event occurs. Read more on [actions](https://zapier.com/help/create/basics/set-up-your-zap-action) on Zapier.

  In this use case, the action will be done on Gameball:

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-137.png" />
  </Frame>

  <AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="A. Choose App & Event" icon="wand-magic-sparkles">
    Choose App as **Gameball** and Action as **"Send Event"**.

    <Note>
    This is the only available event.
    </Note>

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-138.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="B. Choose Gameball Account" icon="key">
    <Note>
    You might need to add your API key. For more info on how to get it, read [Get Your Account Integration Details: API Key and Transaction Key](/product-documentation/admin-settings/get-api-keys).
    </Note>

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-139.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="C. Customize Event" icon="pen-to-square">
    Add the event name, event metadata (if needed), and player email.

    <Note>
    Make sure you add the name in this format: `appname_eventname`. Leave metadata empty if metadata is not needed. Add the player email to choose the player you want to give this reward to.

    If you enter an email for a player that is not already created on Gameball, this event will not be sent and the player will be created on Gameball.
    </Note>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="D. Send Data" icon="paper-plane">
    Click on **Test & Review** or **Test & Continue**.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-140.png" />
    </Frame>

    <Warning>
    Without testing the connection between the two apps and resolving all errors, the trigger event will not be sent to Gameball, and you will not be able to reward your players even if they complete the required action.
    </Warning>
  </Accordion>
  </AccordionGroup>

  Finally, click **Turn on Zap** and move to configure the challenge on Gameball.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-141.png" />
  </Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Configure the Challenge on Gameball">
  Log in to your **Gameball Admin Dashboard**, go to **Programs > Challenges**, and click **Add New Challenge**.

  <AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="A. Set Challenge Type" icon="list-check">
    Choose **Event-based** as the challenge type, and click **Next**.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-142.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="B. Set Challenge Details and Rewards" icon="gift">
    Set the challenge details that will be used internally and displayed to users, including challenge internal name, challenge displayed badge, and displayed information.

    Choose the points reward for this challenge achievement and the score reward (if you have chosen score as your leveling up method). [Learn more](/product-documentation/programs/vip-tiers/tier-settings/tiering-up-methods).

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-143.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="C. Set Challenge Settings" icon="gear">
    Configure the challenge details by clicking the **Edit Icon** next to **Challenge Settings**.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-144.png" />
    </Frame>

    - **Activation Settings:** Is this challenge always active for players to achieve?
    - **Challenge visibility:** Is the challenge displayed on the player widget?
    - **Unlocking settings:** On which level(s) should this challenge be unlocked?
    - **Repeatability Settings:** How many times can this challenge be won?
    - **Player Notification Settings:** Do you want to notify the player when this challenge is achieved?
    - **Event Notification Settings:** Is the player notified after every action taken that leads to achieving the challenge?
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="D. Set Event Settings" icon="bolt">
    From the event list, choose the corresponding **Event Name** you added during the "Customize Event" step. Select it and make sure the number of times this event must be completed is **one**.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-145.png" />
    </Frame>

    Finally, review the previously configured steps, go back to make edits if needed, then click **Save**.
  </Accordion>
  </AccordionGroup>

  Your players will start earning this challenge upon survey form submission.
</Step>
</Steps>
