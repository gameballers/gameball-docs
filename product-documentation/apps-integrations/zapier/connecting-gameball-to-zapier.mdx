Zapier is one of the most popular tools that lets you connect two or more different apps or services to send and receive various types of information between them, and automatically execute actions based on your pre-given instructions. This helps you automate repetitive tasks, saving you time and effort.

If for example, you want Gameball to reward players when they make a specific action on a different app, Zapier is the perfect tool to help with that.

---

## How Does Zapier Work?

If you are connecting two different applications using Zapier, Zapier can execute an action on one of the two applications when a certain trigger happens on the other one. For example, once you receive a certain type of email on Gmail (trigger application), Zapier can then send you a message on Slack notifying you of the email.
