## Why Integrate Omnisend with Gameball?

This integration will allow you to target and segment your customers based on how loyal they are to your app. Specifically, it enables you to:

1. Segment your contact lists based on Gameball customer attributes.
2. Trigger an automation based on Gameball customers' attributes.
3. Send more personalized emails by including Gameball customer information in email templates, for instance, the customer's current tier.

---

## How to Configure Omnisend on Gameball

<Steps>
<Step title="Go to Integrations on Gameball">
  Navigate to **Integrations** on your **Gameball Admin Dashboard**.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-79.png" />
  </Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Connect Omnisend">
  Click **Connect** under **Omnisend**, then install Omnisend from the Shopify App Store.
</Step>

<Step title="Get Your API Key from Omnisend">
  Get your API Key from your Omnisend account through **Store Settings > Integrations & APIs**.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-80.png" />
  </Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Paste the Key into Gameball">
  Copy the API Key into your Gameball admin dashboard. Paste this key and URL to your Gameball account and click **Next**.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-81.png" />
  </Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Add Customer Attributes">
  Click **Add** so this list of customer attributes will be automatically added to your Omnisend account.

  <Note>
  Make sure you use these attributes with the notation provided on your Gameball Admin Dashboard, as described below.
  </Note>

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-82.png" />
  </Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Use Attributes in Omnisend">
  Go to your Omnisend account to check customer contact general details, including Gameball customer profile attributes, and trigger automation. Check the custom properties of your audience.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-83.png" />
  </Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Send Personalized Emails">
  Send personalized emails using Gameball custom attributes by clicking on **Insert Personalization > Contact > Custom Properties > Choose Gameball Custom Property**.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-84.png" />
  </Frame>
</Step>
</Steps>
