## Why Integrate Omnisend with Gameball?

This integration allows you to target and segment your customers based on how loyal they are to your app. With this integration, you can:

- **Segment your contact lists** based on Gameball customer attributes.
- **Trigger automations** based on Gameball customer attributes.
- **Send personalized emails** by including Gameball customer information in email templates, such as the customer's current tier.

---

## How to Configure Omnisend on Gameball

<Steps>
<Step title="Connect Omnisend">
Go to **Integrations** on your **Gameball Admin Dashboard** and click **Connect** under **Omnisend**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-79.png" />
</Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Get Your API Key">
1. Install Omnisend from the Shopify App Store.
2. In your Omnisend account, go to **Store Settings > Integrations & APIs**.
3. Copy the **API Key**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-80.png" />
</Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Paste Credentials in Gameball">
Paste the API key into your Gameball account and click **Next**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-81.png" />
</Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Add Customer Attributes">
Click **Add** so the list of customer attributes will be automatically added to your Omnisend account.

<Note>
Make sure you use these attributes with the notation provided on your Gameball Admin Dashboard, as described below.
</Note>

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-82.png" />
</Frame>
</Step>
</Steps>

## Using Gameball Data in Omnisend

### Check Custom Properties

Go to your Omnisend account to check customer contact details, including Gameball customer profile attributes.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-83.png" />
</Frame>

### Send Personalized Emails

Use Gameball custom attributes by clicking **Insert Personalization > Contact > Custom Properties** and choosing a Gameball custom property.
