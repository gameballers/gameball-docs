### Why should you integrate Omnisend with your Gameball account? And how can you make the best out of Gameball customer attributes?

This integration will allow you to target and segment your customers based on how loyal they are to your app. This integration will allow you to:

1.  Segment your contact lists based on Gameball customer attributes.

2.  Trigger an automation based on Gameball customers' attributes.

3.  Send more personalized emails by including Gameball customer information in email templates, for instance, the customer's current tier.


## How to configure Omnisend on Gameball?

-   Go to **Integrations** on your **Gameball Admin Dashboard**


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-79.png" />
</Frame>

-   Click **Connect** under **Omnisend**


-   Install Omnisend from Shopify App Store

-   Get your API Key from your Omnisend account through Store **Settings > Integrations & APIs**


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-80.png" />
</Frame>

-   Copy the API Key into your Gameball admin dashboard

-   Paste this key and URL to your Gameball account and click **Next**


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-81.png" />
</Frame>

-   Click **Add** so this list of customer attributes will be automatically added to your Omnisend account


**_Note:_** Make sure you use these attributes with the notation provided on your Gameball Admin Dashboard, as described below.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-82.png" />
</Frame>

-   Finally, go to your Omnisend account to check customer contact general details, including Gameball customer profile attributes, and trigger automation.

-   Check the custom properties of your audience.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-83.png" />
</Frame>

-   Send personalized emails using Gameball custom Attributes by clicking on **Insert Personalization > Contact > Custom Properties > Choose Gameball Custom Property**


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-84.png" />
</Frame>
