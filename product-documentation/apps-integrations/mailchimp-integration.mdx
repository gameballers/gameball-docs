## To start off, here is why you should consider integrating Gameball with MailChimp!

This integration will allow you to target and segment your customers based on how loyal they are to your app. This integration will allow you to:

1.  Segment your contact lists based on Gameball customer attributes.

2.  Trigger an automation based on Gameball customer attributes.

3.  Send more personalized emails by including Gameball customer information in email templates, for instance, the customer's current tier.


## How to configure MailChimp on Gameball?

-   Go to **Integrations** on your **Gameball Admin Dashboard**.


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-74.png" />
</Frame>

-   Click on **Connect** under **MailChimp**


-   Install MailChimp from the Shopify App Store

-   Get your API Key from your MailChimp account through **Settings > Integrations**

-   Copy this API key


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-75.png" />
</Frame>

-   Paste this key to your Gameball account and click **Next**


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-76.png" />
</Frame>

-   Choose a list you would like your Gameball customer attributes to be synced to


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-77.png" />
</Frame>

-   Click **Add** so that this list of customer attributes will be automatically added to your MailChimp account


**_Note:_** _Make sure you use these attributes with the notation provided on your Gameball Admin Dashboard, as described below._

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-78.png" />
</Frame>
