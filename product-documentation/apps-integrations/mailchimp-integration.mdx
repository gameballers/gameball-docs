## Why Integrate Mailchimp with Gameball?

This integration allows you to target and segment your customers based on how loyal they are to your app. With this integration, you can:

- **Segment your contact lists** based on Gameball customer attributes.
- **Trigger automations** based on Gameball customer attributes.
- **Send personalized emails** by including Gameball customer information in email templates, such as the customer's current tier.

---

## How to Configure Mailchimp on Gameball

<Steps>
<Step title="Connect Mailchimp">
Go to **Integrations** on your **Gameball Admin Dashboard** and click **Connect** under **Mailchimp**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-74.png" />
</Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Get Your API Key">
1. Install Mailchimp from the Shopify App Store.
2. In your Mailchimp account, go to **Settings > Integrations**.
3. Copy the **API key**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-75.png" />
</Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Paste Credentials in Gameball">
Paste the API key into your Gameball account and click **Next**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-76.png" />
</Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Choose a Contact List">
Choose a list you would like your Gameball customer attributes to be synced to.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-77.png" />
</Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Add Customer Attributes">
Click **Add** so the list of customer attributes will be automatically added to your Mailchimp account.

<Note>
Make sure you use these attributes with the notation provided on your Gameball Admin Dashboard, as described below.
</Note>

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-78.png" />
</Frame>

You're all set! Now segment your contact list and add reward information to your campaigns.
</Step>
</Steps>
