## Why Integrate Gameball with MailChimp?

This integration will allow you to target and segment your customers based on how loyal they are to your app. Specifically, it enables you to:

1. Segment your contact lists based on Gameball customer attributes.
2. Trigger an automation based on Gameball customer attributes.
3. Send more personalized emails by including Gameball customer information in email templates, for instance, the customer's current tier.

---

## How to Configure MailChimp on Gameball

<Steps>
<Step title="Go to Integrations on Gameball">
  Navigate to **Integrations** on your **Gameball Admin Dashboard**.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-74.png" />
  </Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Connect MailChimp">
  Click on **Connect** under **MailChimp**, then install MailChimp from the Shopify App Store.
</Step>

<Step title="Get Your API Key from MailChimp">
  Get your API Key from your MailChimp account through **Settings > Integrations**. Copy this API key.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-75.png" />
  </Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Paste the Key into Gameball">
  Paste this key to your Gameball account and click **Next**.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-76.png" />
  </Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Choose a Sync List">
  Choose a list you would like your Gameball customer attributes to be synced to.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-77.png" />
  </Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Add Customer Attributes">
  Click **Add** so that this list of customer attributes will be automatically added to your MailChimp account.

  <Note>
  Make sure you use these attributes with the notation provided on your Gameball Admin Dashboard, as described below.
  </Note>

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-78.png" />
  </Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Segment and Personalize">
  Finally, segment your contact list and add reward information to your campaign.
</Step>
</Steps>
