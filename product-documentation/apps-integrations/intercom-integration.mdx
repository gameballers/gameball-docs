## Why Integrate Intercom with Gameball?

This integration allows you to target and segment your customers based on how loyal they are to your app. With this integration, you can:

- **Segment your contact lists** based on Gameball customer attributes.
- **Send personalized emails** by including Gameball customer information in email templates.

---

## How to Configure Intercom on Gameball

<Steps>
<Step title="Connect Intercom">
Go to **Apps** on your **Gameball Admin Dashboard** and click **Connect** under **Intercom**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-56.png" />
</Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Get Your API Token">
1. Install Intercom from the Shopify App Store.
2. Go to your **Intercom Developers Hub > Configure > Authentication**.
3. Copy the **API Key**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-57.png" />
</Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Paste Credentials in Gameball">
Paste the API key into your Gameball account and click **Next**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-58.png" />
</Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Add Customer Attributes">
Click **Add** so the list of customer attributes will be automatically added to your Intercom account.

<Note>
Make sure you use these attributes with the notation provided on your Gameball Admin Dashboard, as described below.
</Note>

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-59.png" />
</Frame>
</Step>
</Steps>

---

## Using Gameball Data in Intercom

Once connected, go to your Intercom account to segment lists using Gameball customer profile attributes, send personalized emails with Gameball attributes, and more.
