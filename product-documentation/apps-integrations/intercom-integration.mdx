## Why Integrate Intercom with Gameball?

This integration will allow you to target and segment your customers based on how loyal they are to your app. Specifically, this integration allows you to:

1. Segment your contact lists based on Gameball customer attributes.
2. Send more personalized emails by including Gameball customer information in email templates.

---

## How to Configure Intercom on Gameball

<Steps>
<Step title="Go to Apps on Gameball">
  Navigate to **Apps** on your **Gameball Admin Dashboard**.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-56.png" />
  </Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Connect Intercom">
  Click on **Connect** under **Intercom**, then install Intercom from the Shopify App Store.
</Step>

<Step title="Get Your API Token from Intercom">
  Get your API token by going to your **Intercom Developers Hub > Configure > Authentication**. Copy this API Key.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-57.png" />
  </Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Paste the Key into Gameball">
  Paste this key and URL to your Gameball account and click **Next**.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-58.png" />
  </Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Add Customer Attributes">
  Click **Add** so that this list of customer attributes will be automatically added to your Intercom account.

  <Note>
  Make sure you use these attributes with the notation provided on your Gameball Admin Dashboard, as described below.
  </Note>

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-59.png" />
  </Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Use Attributes in Intercom">
  Finally, go to your Intercom account to segment lists using Gameball Customer Profile Attributes, send personalized emails with Gameball attributes, and more.
</Step>
</Steps>
