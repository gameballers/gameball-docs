### Why should you integrate ActiveCampaign with your Gameball account? And how can you make the best out of Gameball customer attributes?

This integration will allow you to target and segment your customers based on how loyal they are to your app. This integration will allow you to:

1.  Segment your contact lists based on Gameball customer attributes.

2.  Trigger an automation based on Gameball customers' attributes.

3.  Send more personalized emails by including Gameball customer information in email templates, for instance, the customer's current tier.


## How to configure ActiveCampaign on Gameball?

-   Go to **Integrations** on your **Gameball Admin Dashboard**


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-1.png" />
</Frame>

-   Click **Connect** under **ActiveCampaign**


-   Install ActiveCampaign from the Shopify App Store


-   Get your URL and API Key from your ActiveCampaign account through **Settings > Developer**


-   Copy the URL and API Key


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-2.png" />
</Frame>

-   Paste this key and URL to your Gameball account and click **Next**


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-3.png" />
</Frame>

-   Click **Add** so this list of customer Attributes will be automatically added to your ActiveCampaign account
