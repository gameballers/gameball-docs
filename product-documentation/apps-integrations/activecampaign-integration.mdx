---
title: "How Do I Integrate ActiveCampaign with Gameball?"
description: "Keep your customers in sync across Gameball and ActiveCampaign, and target them based on your loyalty data."
---

Integrating ActiveCampaign with Gameball allows you to target and segment your customers based on how loyal they are to your app.

## Why Integrate ActiveCampaign with Gameball?

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Segment Contact Lists" icon="filter">
    Segment your contact lists based on Gameball customer attributes.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Trigger Automations" icon="bolt">
    Trigger an automation based on Gameball customers' attributes.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Personalized Emails" icon="envelope">
    Send more personalized emails by including Gameball customer information in email templates, such as the customer's current tier.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

---

## How to Configure ActiveCampaign on Gameball

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Integrations">
    Go to **Integrations** on your **Gameball Admin Dashboard**.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-1.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect ActiveCampaign">
    Click **Connect** under **ActiveCampaign**. Install ActiveCampaign from the Shopify App Store.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Get Your API Credentials">
    Get your URL and API Key from your ActiveCampaign account through **Settings > Developer**. Copy the URL and API Key.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-2.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Paste Credentials into Gameball">
    Paste the key and URL into your Gameball account and click **Next**.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-3.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Customer Attributes">
    Click **Add** so this list of customer attributes will be automatically added to your ActiveCampaign account.

    <Note>
      Make sure you use these attributes with the notation provided on your Gameball Admin Dashboard, as described below.
    </Note>

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-4.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Use Gameball Data in ActiveCampaign">
    Go to your ActiveCampaign account to check customer contact general details, including Gameball customer Profile Attributes, trigger automation, send personalized emails with Gameball attributes, and more.

    Check the custom fields of a customer:

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-5.png" />
    </Frame>

    While creating a campaign, segment customers based on a Gameball customer profile attribute:

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-6.png" />
    </Frame>

    Split an automation based on Gameball customer profile attributes:

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-7.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>
