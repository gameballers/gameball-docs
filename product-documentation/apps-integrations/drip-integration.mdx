### Why should you integrate Drip with your Gameball account? And how can you make the best out of Gameball customer attributes?

This integration will allow you to target and segment your customers based on how loyal they are to your app. This integration will allow you to:

1.  Check Gameball customer attributes.

2.  Send more personalized emails by including Gameball customer information in email templates, for instance, the customer's current tier.


## How to configure Drip on Gameball?

-   Go to **Integrations** on your **Gameball Admin Dashboard**


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-21.png" />
</Frame>

-   Click **Connect** under **Drip**


-   Install Drip from Shopify App Store

-   Get your URL and API Key from your Drip account through **Settings > Developer**

-   Copy the URL and API Key

-   Paste this key and URL to your Gameball account and click **Next**


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-22.png" />
</Frame>

-   Click **Add** so this list of customer attributes will be automatically added to your Drip account

-   Finally, go to your Drip account to check the customer profile custom tag through **People > Choose customer**


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-23.png" />
</Frame>

-   Personalize E-mail campaigns by going to **Personalize** > choose one of Gameball's custom profile attributes

</Frame>
