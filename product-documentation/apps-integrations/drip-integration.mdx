description: "Keep your customers in sync across Gameball and Drip, and target them based on your loyalty data."
---

Integrating Drip with Gameball allows you to target and segment your customers based on how loyal they are to your app.

## Why Integrate Drip with Gameball?

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Check Customer Attributes" icon="user-check">
    View Gameball customer attributes directly in your Drip account.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Personalized Emails" icon="envelope">
    Send more personalized emails by including Gameball customer information in email templates, such as the customer's current tier.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

---

## How to Configure Drip on Gameball

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Integrations">
    Go to **Integrations** on your **Gameball Admin Dashboard**.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-21.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect Drip">
    Click **Connect** under **Drip**. Install Drip from the Shopify App Store.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Get Your API Credentials">
    Get your URL and API Key from your Drip account through **Settings > Developer**. Copy the URL and API Key.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Paste Credentials into Gameball">
    Paste the key and URL into your Gameball account and click **Next**.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-22.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add Customer Attributes">
    Click **Add** so this list of customer attributes will be automatically added to your Drip account.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify in Drip">
    Go to your Drip account to check the customer profile custom tag through **People > Choose customer**.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-23.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Personalize Email Campaigns">
    Personalize email campaigns by going to **Personalize** and choosing one of Gameball's custom profile attributes.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-24.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>
