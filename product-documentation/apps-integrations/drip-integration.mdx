## Why Integrate Drip with Gameball?

This integration allows you to target and segment your customers based on how loyal they are to your app. With this integration, you can:

- **Check Gameball customer attributes** directly in Drip.
- **Send personalized emails** by including Gameball customer information in email templates, such as the customer's current tier.

---

## How to Configure Drip on Gameball

<Steps>
<Step title="Connect Drip">
Go to **Integrations** on your **Gameball Admin Dashboard** and click **Connect** under **Drip**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-21.png" />
</Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Get Your API Credentials">
1. Install Drip from the Shopify App Store.
2. In your Drip account, go to **Settings > Developer**.
3. Copy the **URL** and **API Key**.
</Step>

<Step title="Paste Credentials in Gameball">
Paste the URL and API Key into your Gameball account and click **Next**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-22.png" />
</Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Add Customer Attributes">
Click **Add** so the list of customer attributes will be automatically added to your Drip account.
</Step>
</Steps>

---

## Using Gameball Data in Drip

Once connected, go to your Drip account to leverage Gameball loyalty data.

### Check Customer Tags

View Gameball customer profile custom tags through **People > Choose customer**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-23.png" />
</Frame>

### Personalize Email Campaigns

Go to **Personalize** and choose one of Gameball's custom profile attributes.

  <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-24.png" />
</Frame>
