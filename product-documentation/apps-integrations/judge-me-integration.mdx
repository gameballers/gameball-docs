### Why should you integrate Judge.me with your Gameball account? And how can you make the reviews through Gameball?

This integration will allow you to reward your customers based on their placed reviews, hence, more reviews for your business, thus, more trust by your customers to grow your business.

**_❗️❗️Important note:_** _to integrate Gameball with Judge.me, You have to be on the "**Awesome**" plan on Judge.me._

### How to configure Judge.me on Gameball?

-   Go to **Integrations** on your **Gameball Admin Dashboard**


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-60.png" />
</Frame>

-   Click **Connect** under **Judge.me**


-   Install Judge.me from Shopify App Store

-   Get your API Key from your Judge.me account through **Settings > Integrations**

-   Copy the API token


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-61.png" />
</Frame>

-   Paste this key to your Gameball account and click **Verify Key**


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-62.png" />
</Frame>

-   Click **Add** to automatically add the review event in the customers' events section on your Gameball Admin Dashboard


<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-63.png" />
</Frame>

-   Finally, go to your **Rewards > Reward Campaigns** and create the "review/judge me" campaign.


**_Note:_** _This event will have the following metadata: Product ID, Product Title, and Rating._

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-64.png" />
</Frame>

Learn more about how to [configure your reward campaign](/product-documentation/rewards-campaigns/start-from-scratch/create-action-based-reward-campaign).
