## Why Integrate Judge.me with Gameball?

This integration will allow you to reward your customers based on their placed reviews, generating more reviews for your business and building greater trust to grow your audience.

<Warning>
To integrate Gameball with Judge.me, you must be on the **"Awesome"** plan on Judge.me.
</Warning>

---

## How to Configure Judge.me on Gameball

<Steps>
<Step title="Go to Integrations on Gameball">
  Navigate to **Integrations** on your **Gameball Admin Dashboard**.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-60.png" />
  </Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Connect Judge.me">
  Click **Connect** under **Judge.me**, then install Judge.me from the Shopify App Store.
</Step>

<Step title="Get Your API Key from Judge.me">
  Get your API Key from your Judge.me account through **Settings > Integrations**. Copy the API token.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-61.png" />
  </Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Verify the Key in Gameball">
  Paste this key into your Gameball account and click **Verify Key**.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-62.png" />
  </Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Add the Review Event">
  Click **Add** to automatically add the review event in the customers' events section on your Gameball Admin Dashboard.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-63.png" />
  </Frame>
</Step>

<Step title="Create the Review Reward Campaign">
  Go to **Rewards > Reward Campaigns** and create the "review/judge me" campaign.

  <Note>
  This event will have the following metadata: Product ID, Product Title, and Rating.
  </Note>

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-64.png" />
  </Frame>

  Learn more about how to [configure your reward campaign](/product-documentation/rewards-campaigns/start-from-scratch/create-action-based-reward-campaign).
</Step>
</Steps>
