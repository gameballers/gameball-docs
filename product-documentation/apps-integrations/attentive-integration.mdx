Gameball and Attentive integration allows you to leverage customer engagement data from Gameball to power your SMS marketing campaigns in Attentive. Follow this guide to seamlessly connect the two platforms and maximize the impact of your loyalty and marketing efforts.

---

## Step 1: Prerequisites

Before starting the integration, ensure the following:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Active Gameball Account" icon="gamepad">
    You have an active Gameball account ready to connect.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Active Attentive Account" icon="mobile">
    You have an active Attentive account for SMS campaigns.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Admin Permissions" icon="shield-check">
    You have admin permissions in both platforms.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

---

## Step 2: Enable Gameball API Access

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to Your Gameball Dashboard">
    Log in to your **Gameball Dashboard** and navigate to the **Integrations** section from the left menu.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Connect Attentive">
    Select **Attentive** and click **Connect**, then **Next**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Authorize in Attentive">
    You will be redirected to the **Attentive dashboard**. Scroll down and click **Authorize**.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm Connection">
    Go back to the integration section on the dashboard to ensure that the Attentive app is now connected to your Gameball account.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/product-docs/apps-integrations/apps-integrations-25.png" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Synced Customer Data

The following attributes are automatically synced from Gameball to Attentive:

| Attribute | Description |
|---|---|
| Customer ID | Unique customer identifier |
| Date of Birth | Customer's birthday |
| Join Date | Date the customer joined |
| Tier Name | Current loyalty tier name |
| Wallet Points | Current points balance |
| Pending Points | Points awaiting confirmation |
| Tier Progress | Progress toward next tier |
| Tier ID | Current tier identifier |
| Completed Reward Campaigns | Campaigns the customer has achieved |
| Referral Code | Customer's unique referral code |
| Referral Link | Customer's shareable referral link |
| Tags and Segments | Customer tags and segment membership |

<Steps>
  <Step title="Save Your Settings">
    After reviewing the attributes, save your settings to begin syncing.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Test the Integration

Before launching your campaigns, test the integration to ensure it works as expected:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Perform a Test Action in Gameball">
    Award points to a test user or trigger a loyalty event.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify in Attentive">
    Check if the event appears in Attentive under the corresponding profile.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Send a Test SMS">
    Send a test SMS using the synced data in Attentive to confirm the data appears correctly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

---

## Create SMS Campaigns in Attentive

Now that the integration is live, use Gameball data to personalize your Attentive campaigns:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Campaigns">
    Navigate to **Campaigns** in Attentive.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Create a New Campaign">
    Create a new campaign and select your target audience based on Gameball data (e.g., customers who achieved a certain tier or redeemed rewards).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Personalize Your Messages">
    Use dynamic variables (e.g., `{total_points}`, `{current_level}`) to personalize your messages.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Launch Your Campaign">
    Review your campaign settings and launch your campaign.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="API key or webhook URL not configured correctly" icon="triangle-exclamation">
    Verify that the API key and webhook URL are correctly configured in both Gameball and Attentive settings.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Integration errors appearing in logs" icon="bug">
    Check the integration logs in Gameball for error messages and review the error details to identify the cause.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Need additional support" icon="headset">
    Contact Gameball support or Attentive support for further assistance with your integration.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
