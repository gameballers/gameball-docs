### Why should you integrate Segment with your Gameball account? And how can you make the best out of Gameball customer attributes?

This integration will allow you to target and segment your customers based on how loyal they are to your app. This integration will allow you to:

1.  Segment your contact lists based on Gameball customer attributes.

2.  Trigger an automation based on Gameball customers' attributes.

3.  Send more personalized emails by including Gameball customer information in email templates, for instance, the customer's current tier.
