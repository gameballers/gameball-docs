Customer segmentation enables you to target specific groups of customers for personalized communication, reward campaigns, and more. With Gameball, segmentation is dynamic and automatic, saving you the time and effort of manually grouping customers and keeping your segments up to date.

---

## The Problem Segmentation Solves

Have you ever wanted to create a challenge, send a message, or apply a redemption rule that only applies to a specific group of customers — but could not figure out how to isolate and target them accurately? Even when you managed to identify these customers, applying manual tags became time-consuming and inaccurate, especially since customers' behavior changes frequently. By the time you have tagged a customer, they may no longer belong to the intended group.

To solve this challenge, Gameball introduced **Customer Segmentation** — a feature that creates dynamic lists of customers who automatically qualify based on the criteria you set.

---

## How Customer Segmentation Works

Customer segmentation automatically groups your customers based on their behavior and profile data. Unlike manual tags, segments are dynamic: customers are added or removed in real time based on whether they meet the defined conditions.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/customer-segmentation/segmentation-dynamic-grouping.gif" alt="Segmentation dynamic grouping" />
</Frame>

When a customer meets all the conditions of a segment, they are automatically added to it. If their data changes and no longer matches those conditions, they are automatically removed.

## Existing Segments vs. Custom Segments

Gameball provides two built-in segments that help you monitor essential customer behaviors:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Active Users">
    - Last login: within the last 30 days
    - OR last order date: within the last 30 days
    - **Color:** Green
    - **Segment Tag:** Active
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Inactive Users">
    - Last login: more than 60 days ago OR login is empty
    - Last order date: more than 90 days ago
    - **Color:** Orange
    - **Segment Tag:** Inactive
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

In addition, you can create, edit, or delete your own segments based on your business needs.

## How to Create Segments

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Gameball Dashboard">
    Go to the **Gameball Admin Dashboard**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Segments">
    Navigate to **Customers** > **Segments**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create a New Segment">
    Click **Create New Segment**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Define Conditions">
    Define the conditions for your segment. For example:

    - Female customers
    - Active in the past 30 days
    - Frequently visit a specific product category
  </Step>
</Steps>

Once the segment is saved, Gameball automatically identifies and groups customers who meet all of these criteria.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/customer-segmentation/create-new-segment.gif" alt="Creating a new segment" />
</Frame>

For more details on how to use the segmentation engine and understand the available operators, refer to [How to Segment Customers](/product-documentation/customer-segmentation/how-to-segment-customers).

## Segment Usage in Automations

Each segment shows which automation campaigns use it as a trigger — specifically whether it's used as an **Entered Segment** or **Exited Segment** trigger — along with each automation's live status.

This gives you full visibility before making changes, so you can avoid accidentally breaking active automations when editing or deleting a segment.

<Frame caption="Segment usage panel showing automation campaigns and their live status">
  ![Segment usage in automations](/images/product-docs/customer-segmentation/segment-usage-automations.png)
</Frame>

<Info>
If a segment is used by one or more active automations, you'll see them listed directly on the segment's page. Review these dependencies before making any structural changes to your segment conditions.
</Info>

## Common Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can I use segments across all Gameball features?">
    Yes, segments can be used with challenges, messages, and redemption rules.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Will customers be added back to a segment if they meet the criteria again?">
    Yes, customers are added and removed dynamically in real time.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I edit or delete existing segments?">
    Yes, you can modify or delete both system-generated and custom segments at any time.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How is segmentation different from tags?">
    Tags are added manually and stay fixed. Segments are dynamic — Gameball constantly checks if customers meet the conditions and updates the list automatically.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What is dynamic segmentation?">
    It means the segment updates in real time. Customers can enter or leave a segment at any time based on their current data.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What does real-time segmentation mean?">
    Gameball instantly scans your customer base and updates segments the moment you create or change the rules.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
