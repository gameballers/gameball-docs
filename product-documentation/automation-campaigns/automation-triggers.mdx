Automation triggers serve as the backbone of efficient workflows, enabling businesses to automate tasks seamlessly. In this article, we delve into the various automation triggers offered by Gameball, exploring their functionalities and applications in detail.

## Exploring Automation Campaign Triggers

Automation triggers are the catalysts that initiate predefined actions within workflows. Understanding these triggers is essential for maximizing the efficiency of automation campaigns.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/automation-campaigns/triggers-panel.png" alt="Automation triggers panel" />
</Frame>

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Account Created" icon="user-plus">
    Triggered once a customer creates an account, the Account Created trigger marks the beginning of a user's journey with your business. This trigger presents an opportunity for you to initiate onboarding processes, welcome new users, and provide valuable information or incentives to encourage further engagement.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/product-docs/automation-campaigns/trigger-account-created.png" alt="Account Created trigger configuration" />
    </Frame>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="On Date Trigger" icon="calendar">
    Start automation on a specific date/time with the OnDate Trigger. This feature allows businesses to schedule automation campaigns in advance, ensuring the timely execution of tasks and communications. For example, Black Friday, flash sale, brand anniversary, etc.

    ### How to set up the On Date trigger

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Open Date Configuration">
        Click on **Set Date** on the card.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select an Option">
        From the side pop-up, **Select an Option (Birthday, Join Date, or Custom)**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Select the Date/Time">
        If you chose custom, then select the **Date/Time**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Save">
        Press **Save**.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/product-docs/automation-campaigns/trigger-on-date.png" alt="On Date trigger configuration" />
    </Frame>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Received an Event" icon="bolt">
    This trigger is activated when a customer completes a specific action on the platform. Whether it's making a purchase, writing a review, filling out a survey, viewing a product, or engaging with content, the Receive Event trigger allows you to respond promptly to user interactions.

    ### How to set up the Received an Event trigger

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Add Event">
        Click on **Add Event** on the card.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Choose Event">
        From the side pop-up, **Choose Event**.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Apply Filters">
        **Apply** the **Filters** you need.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Save">
        Press **Save**.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/product-docs/automation-campaigns/trigger-received-event.png" alt="Received an Event trigger configuration" />
    </Frame>

    <Note>
      When selecting "Receive Event," you'll need to pick the exact event criteria using the Event Engine.
    </Note>
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Automation Completed" icon="check-circle">
    The **Automation Completed** trigger helps you to seamlessly link multiple automations, ensuring a smooth transition from one workflow to the next. When a customer completes an automation, you can **automatically trigger the next one**, eliminating manual intervention and enhancing engagement, ensuring a fluid, connected customer journey, allowing you to build dynamic, multi-stage automation workflows that drive engagement and retention.

    ### Use Cases

    <CardGroup cols={3}>
      <Card title="Progressive Onboarding" icon="stairs">
        Guide new users through a structured onboarding experience by triggering the next step upon completion of the previous one. For example, once a customer completes their profile setup, they can be automatically enrolled in a tutorial about earning rewards.
      </Card>
      <Card title="Tier-Based Rewards" icon="layer-group">
        Encourage deeper engagement by unlocking exclusive benefits as customers progress. After a customer completes a milestone (e.g., making their first purchase), they can be automatically added to a VIP automation that grants them bonus rewards.
      </Card>
      <Card title="Reactivation Campaigns" icon="rotate">
        Keep customers engaged with a multi-step retention strategy. If a customer completes a re-engagement campaign, they can be seamlessly transitioned into a follow-up automation that offers a personalized discount or gamified challenge.
      </Card>
    </CardGroup>
  </Accordion>
