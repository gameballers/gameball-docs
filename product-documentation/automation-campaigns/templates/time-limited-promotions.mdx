This article covers the **Time-Limited Promotions** automation templates designed to drive sales and boost customer engagement.

---

## Black Friday Template

### Goal

Capitalize on Black Friday with limited-time discounts.

### Flow

On Black Friday, this automation provides a 10% discount valid for 7 days. Customers are notified about the discount via email and the automation waits for them to place an order. If no order is made, a reminder email is sent one day before the discount expires.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/automation-campaigns/templates/black-friday.png" alt="Black Friday automation flow" />
</Frame>

### Trigger

On Date (November 29)

### Instructions

The date (November 29) is already pre-selected on the trigger block — you are good to go!

## Flash Sale Template

### Goal

Drive sales with flash sale discounts and reminders.

### Flow

On a set date, this automation activates a 20% discount for 7 days, communicated via email. It then waits for the customer to place an order. If no order is placed, a reminder email is sent one day before the discount expires.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/automation-campaigns/templates/flash-sale.png" alt="Flash Sale automation flow" />
</Frame>

### Trigger

On Date

### Instructions

Select the desired date from the trigger block, and you are all set!

## Double Points on Weekends Template

### Goal

### Flow

## Points Expiry Reminder

### Goal

Gives you full control over **when and how to notify customers about their expiring points**, helping you drive engagement while reducing liability.

### Flow

This automation is triggered specifically when a customer's points are near expiration, sending them any type of chosen notification.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/automation-campaigns/templates/points-expiry-reminder.png" alt="Points Expiry Reminder automation flow" />
</Frame>

### Trigger

**"Points to Expire"**

- Available in the **Automation Builder**.
- Detects when a customer's points are **approaching expiration**.

### Instructions

<Steps>
  <Step title="Define timeframe">
    Set a **custom timeframe** (e.g., 30, 15, 7, or X days before expiry).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose notification actions">
    Select one or more actions to notify customers:
    - Send Email
    - Send SMS
    - Send Push Notification
    - Send Webhook
  </Step>
</Steps>
