Encouraging repeat purchases is vital for sustaining business growth and fostering customer loyalty. In this article, we'll dive into more details and discover the **Encourage Repurchase** templates designed to boost your sales.

---

## First Purchase Upsell

### Goal

Turn your one-time buyers into repeat customers with an enticing 10% offer to encourage them to shop again. This offer includes a 14-day waiting period to ensure customers receive their orders.

<Frame>
  ![First Purchase Upsell automation flow](/images/product-docs/automation-campaigns/templates/first-purchase-upsell.png)
</Frame>

### Trigger

Placed order

### Instructions

<Note>
- Consider turning off any other active upsell automation for customers who have placed only one order to prevent duplicate emails from being sent.
- Adjust the 14-day wait to match your process. It's set to ensure that the customer received the order; change it if needed.
</Note>

## Customer Win-Back

Re-engage inactive customers. If the customer becomes inactive (no logins for the last 60 days or no orders for the last 90 days), we offer them a 20% discount valid for 14 days, communicated via email. Additionally, we send a reminder two days before the offer expires.

<Frame>
  ![Customer Win-Back automation flow](/images/product-docs/automation-campaigns/templates/customer-win-back.png)
</Frame>

### Trigger

Segment Entered (Inactive Customers)

### Instructions

- You're good to go!

## [Shopify] Abandoned Checkout

Encourage customers who abandoned their checkout to complete their purchases by creating a sense of urgency. When a customer starts checkout, we wait for 2 hours to receive an order placement. If not, we offer them a 10% discount valid for 2 days to create a sense of urgency and encourage them to complete the purchase.

<Frame>
  ![Shopify Abandoned Checkout automation flow](/images/product-docs/automation-campaigns/templates/shopify-abandoned-checkout.png)
</Frame>

### Trigger

Checkout started

### Instructions

<Warning>
Only Shopify users can access this template.
</Warning>

---

## [Self Serve] Abandoned Cart

Encourage customers who abandoned their carts to complete their purchases by creating a sense of urgency. When a customer adds a product to his cart, we wait for 2 hours to receive an order placement. If not, we offer them a free shipping coupon valid for 2 days to create a sense of urgency and encourage them to complete the purchase.

<Frame>
  ![Self Serve Abandoned Cart automation flow](/images/product-docs/automation-campaigns/templates/self-serve-abandoned-cart.png)
</Frame>

### Trigger

Add to Cart

### Instructions

- You're good to go!
