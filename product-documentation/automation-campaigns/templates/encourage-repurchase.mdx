Encouraging repeat purchases is vital for sustaining business growth and fostering customer loyalty. This article covers the **Encourage Repurchase** automation templates designed to boost your sales.

---

## First Purchase Upsell

### Goal

Turn one-time buyers into repeat customers with an enticing 10% offer to encourage them to shop again. This offer includes a 14-day waiting period to ensure customers receive their orders.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/automation-campaigns/templates/first-purchase-upsell.png" alt="First Purchase Upsell automation flow" />
</Frame>

### Trigger

Placed Order

### Instructions

<Note>
  - Consider turning off any other active upsell automation for customers who have placed only one order to prevent duplicate emails from being sent.
  - Adjust the 14-day wait to match your process. It is set to ensure that the customer received the order; change it if needed.
</Note>

---

## Customer Win-Back

Re-engage inactive customers. If the customer becomes inactive (no logins for the last 60 days or no orders for the last 90 days), they are offered a 20% discount valid for 14 days, communicated via email. A reminder is also sent two days before the offer expires.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/automation-campaigns/templates/customer-win-back.png" alt="Customer Win-Back automation flow" />
</Frame>

### Trigger

Segment Entered (Inactive Customers)

### Instructions

You are good to go!

## [Shopify] Abandoned Checkout

Encourage customers who abandoned their checkout to complete their purchases by creating a sense of urgency. When a customer starts checkout, the automation waits 2 hours for an order placement. If no order is placed, a 10% discount valid for 2 days is offered to create urgency and encourage the customer to complete the purchase.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/automation-campaigns/templates/shopify-abandoned-checkout.png" alt="Shopify Abandoned Checkout automation flow" />
</Frame>

### Trigger

Checkout Started

### Instructions

<Warning>
  Only Shopify users can access this template.
</Warning>

## [Self Serve] Abandoned Cart

Encourage customers who abandoned their carts to complete their purchases by creating a sense of urgency. When a customer adds a product to their cart, the automation waits 2 hours for an order placement. If no order is placed, a free shipping coupon valid for 2 days is offered to encourage the customer to complete the purchase.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/automation-campaigns/templates/self-serve-abandoned-cart.png" alt="Self Serve Abandoned Cart automation flow" />
</Frame>

### Trigger

Add to Cart

### Instructions

You are good to go!
