These templates are designed to empower our automation modules, providing a set of predefined workflows to showcase to customers how they can leverage automation effectively.

### Time-Limited Promotions

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/automation-campaigns/templates/time-limited-promotions-overview.png" alt="Time-Limited Promotions templates overview" />
</Frame>

- **Black Friday:** Capitalize on Black Friday with a limited-time 10% discount communicated via email.
- **Flash Sale:** Drive sales with a 10% discount for 7 days, communicated via email.
- **Double Points on Weekends:** Encourage weekend purchases with double points offered only during weekends, communicated via email.

### Acquire New Customers

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/automation-campaigns/templates/acquire-new-customers-overview.png" alt="Acquire New Customers templates overview" />
</Frame>

- **Welcome Series:** Welcome new customers with a 20% discount upon account creation. Follow up with a reminder email if they don't purchase within 3 days.
- **Ask Repeated Customers for Referrals:** Encourage loyal customers to refer friends after a purchase. Send a referral email with rewards and a reminder if no referrals are received within 2 days.

[Dive into more details about Acquire New Customers Templates.](/product-documentation/automation-campaigns/templates/acquire-new-customers)

### Encourage Repeated Purchases

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/automation-campaigns/templates/encourage-repurchase-overview-1.png" alt="Encourage Repeated Purchases templates overview" />
</Frame>

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/automation-campaigns/templates/encourage-repurchase-overview-2.png" alt="Encourage Repeated Purchases templates overview continued" />
</Frame>

- **First Purchase Upsell:** Offer one-time buyers a 10% discount to encourage repeat purchases with a 14-day waiting period.
- **Customer Win-back:** Offer inactive customers a 20% discount for 14 days to encourage them to return and make a purchase.
- **Abandoned Cart:** Encourage customers who abandoned their carts to complete their purchases with a 10% discount valid for 2 days.
- **Abandoned Checkout:** Encourage customers who abandoned their order at checkout to complete their purchases with a 10% discount valid for 2 days.
- **Abandoned Browse:** Offer a 10% discount valid for 2 days to customers who visited a product page but didn't add items to their cart within 2 hours.

[Dive into more details about Encourage Repeated Purchases Templates.](/product-documentation/automation-campaigns/templates/encourage-repurchase)

### Boost Sales & Upselling

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/automation-campaigns/templates/boost-sales-upselling-overview.png" alt="Boost Sales and Upselling templates overview" />
</Frame>

- **Promote Product Cross-Sell:** Suggest related products to customers based on their purchase, enhancing their shopping experience and potentially increasing sales.
- **Promote Collection Cross-Sell:** Encourage customers who bought from one collection to explore another, offering a 20% discount on the complementary collection.
- **Increase Average Order Value (AOV):** Offer tiered discounts based on purchase amount to encourage customers to increase their order value.
- **Promote New Arrivals:** Promote new arrivals to customers and encourage purchases from specific collections with timely emails and follow-ups.

[Dive into more details about Boost Sales & Upselling Templates.](/product-documentation/automation-campaigns/templates/boost-sales-and-upselling)

### Reviews & Data Collection

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/automation-campaigns/templates/reviews-data-collection-overview.png" alt="Reviews and Data Collection templates overview" />
</Frame>

- **Ask for Reviews:** Incentivize customers to leave reviews by offering incentives like points, sent 14 days after their purchase.
- **Reward for Reviews:** Reward customers for writing reviews with 200 points and a special badge as recognition, triggered by a webhook when a user writes a review.
- **Collect Feedback/Survey:** Incentivize feedback participation and survey completion with 200 points and a special badge as recognition, triggered by a webhook when a customer leaves feedback or fills out a survey.

## Benefits of Ready-to-Use Templates

- **Time-Saving**: Eliminate the need to create workflows from scratch by leveraging pre-designed templates tailored to specific business objectives.
- **Effortless Implementation:** Easily integrate these templates into your existing automation modules with minimal setup required.
- **Enhanced Customer Engagement:** Deliver personalized and targeted messages to customers at various stages of their journey, fostering stronger relationships and driving conversions.
- **Improved ROI:** Maximize the effectiveness of your automation efforts with proven templates designed to achieve specific objectives, ultimately increasing revenue and ROI.

By leveraging these templates, businesses can optimize their automation efforts, drive meaningful interactions with customers, and achieve their business goals effectively and efficiently.
