This article covers the **Acquire New Customers** automation templates, designed to drive first purchases and encourage loyal customers to spread the word.

---

## Welcome Series Template

### Goal

Encourage new customers to complete their first purchase by giving them a surprise gift — a 20% discount — when they create an account.

### Flow

They receive an email about the discount right away, and if they do not make a purchase within 3 days, they get a reminder email encouraging them to take advantage of the discount.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/automation-campaigns/templates/welcome-series-flow.png" alt="Welcome Series automation flow" />
</Frame>

### Trigger

Account Created

### Instructions

<Warning>
  - Avoid using the "Create Account" challenge with this automation to prevent customers from earning rewards twice.
  - Make sure to disable any other welcome email upon account creation to prevent duplicate emails from being sent.
</Warning>

---

## Ask Repeated Customers for Referrals Template

### Goal

Encourage loyal customers (those with more than 2 orders) to refer their friends after making a purchase.

### Flow

This automation triggers when a customer places an order and waits for 14 days. It then sends an email inviting the customer to refer a friend and earn rewards. If no successful referrals are received within 2 days, a reminder email is sent.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/automation-campaigns/templates/ask-referrals-flow.png" alt="Ask Repeated Customers for Referrals automation flow" />
</Frame>

### Trigger

Placed Order

### Instructions

<Note>
  - Enable the referral program and set up rewards for both your customers and their friends.
  - Ensure that you activate the notification emails for both the customer and their friend upon successful referral.
  - Adjust the 14-day wait to match your process. It is set to ensure that the customer received the order; change it if needed.
</Note>
