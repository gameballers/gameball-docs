## Configure Gameball Time Zone Settings

You can access the **Time zone** settings section in **Gameball Admin Dashboard** through **Settings > Admin Settings > General > Time zone Section**.

<Frame>
  ![Time Zone Settings](/images/product-docs/admin-settings/time-zone-settings.png)
</Frame>

The time zone will be applied to all dates across the system:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Analytics Dashboards" icon="chart-line">
    All date-based analytics and reporting reflect the configured time zone.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Customers Creation Date" icon="user-plus">
    Customer join dates are recorded using the configured time zone.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Programs Achievements" icon="trophy">
    Achievement timestamps are aligned with your selected time zone.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Points Transactions" icon="coins">
    All points earning and redemption events are timestamped in your time zone.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Warning>
Setting the correct time zone is essential to ensure your reports and activity logs reflect accurate timestamps.
</Warning>
