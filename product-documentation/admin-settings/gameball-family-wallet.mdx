**Gameball's Family Wallet** allows customers to link their loyalty accounts with family members or a group, combining all earned points into a single shared wallet. Everyone contributes to the same balance and any member can redeem from it — making it easier for groups to reach rewards faster.

---

## Family Wallet Roles

Every Family Wallet has two types of participants:

| Role | Permissions |
|---|---|
| **Parent (Family Owner)** | Invites members, removes members, deletes the wallet, views full combined points history |
| **Member** | Earns and redeems from the shared wallet, views own points history, can leave at any time |

There is always one Parent per Family Wallet — the customer who created it. From your Gameball dashboard, you can transfer the Parent role to a different customer at any time.

---

## Earning, Redeeming, and Viewing Points

**Earning** — Points earned by the Parent and all members across all Gameball programs and modules are combined into the shared wallet. Groups accumulate points faster than they would individually.

**Redeeming** — Both the Parent and members can redeem from the combined wallet at any time, with no approval required from other members.

**Viewing the wallet** — Each member can see their individual points alongside the combined family balance. The Parent sees the full activity history; members see only their own.

## Enabling the Family Wallet

The Family Wallet is an add-on feature. Once purchased, you can enable it from your dashboard:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Settings">
    In your Gameball dashboard, navigate to **Settings**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the Customers section">
    Under **Customers**, locate the **Family Wallet** option.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable the feature">
    Click the toggle to enable Family Wallet for your customers.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Once enabled, customers can create and join Family Wallets directly from the widget.

## Important Notes

<Note>
**Maximum size:** A Family Wallet can have a maximum of 10 members, including the Parent.
</Note>

<Warning>
**Parent account deletion:** If the Parent's account is deleted, the entire Family Wallet is deleted. All members revert to standard individual accounts and their points are no longer pooled.
</Warning>

<Note>
**Changing the Parent:** If you need to transfer ownership to a different customer, a dashboard admin can make that change directly from the customer profile.
</Note>

## Common Questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Can a customer be part of multiple Family Wallets?">
    No. Each customer can belong to only one Family Wallet at a time, either as a Parent or as a Member.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens when a member leaves the Family Wallet?">
    The member's account reverts to an individual account. Points they earned while in the Family Wallet remain in the shared wallet and are not returned to them.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can members redeem points without the Parent's approval?">
    Yes. Both the Parent and members can redeem from the combined wallet at any time without requiring approval from one another.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens to the Family Wallet if the Parent account is deleted?">
    The Family Wallet is deleted and all members revert to standard individual accounts.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How do I get the Family Wallet for my store?">
    The Family Wallet is an add-on feature. Contact [Gameball support](mailto:support@gameball.co) to add it to your plan.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
