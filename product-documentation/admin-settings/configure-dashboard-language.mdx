## Changing the Dashboard Language

By default, your dashboard language is English (EN). You can easily change that by following these steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in to your Gameball dashboard">
    Access your account at the Gameball Admin Dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Language Settings">
    Go to **Settings** > **Admin Settings** > **General**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit the Language">
    In the **Admin Dashboard Language**, click on the **Edit** icon.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your preferred language">
    Choose your preferred language from the dropdown menu, then click **Save**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the change">
    Watch your dashboard change its previous language to the selected language.
  </Step>
</Steps>
