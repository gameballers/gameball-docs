You can customize the features displayed to your customers on the Gameball widget with ease. Follow these simple steps to access the General Settings of the Gameball Widget:

<Steps>
<Step title="Go to the Gameball Admin Dashboard">
  Log in to the **Gameball Admin Dashboard**.
</Step>

<Step title="Navigate to Widget General Settings">
  From the side menu, navigate to **Widget**, then click on **General**.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/product-docs/customer-widget/widget-general-section.png" alt="Widget general section" />
  </Frame>
</Step>
</Steps>

## Visibility

Manage the overall visibility of the widget, including specific tabs and sections. Access these settings under the **Visibility** tab in the **General** section.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/customer-widget/visibility-tab-general.png" alt="Visibility tab in general section" />
</Frame>

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Widget Visibility" icon="eye">
  Determine who can see the Gameball customer widget, differentiating between Customers and Guests:

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/product-docs/customer-widget/widget-visibility-customers-guests.png" alt="Widget visibility for customers and guests" />
  </Frame>

  - **Guests**: A visitor to your website who does not have an account or has not logged in.
  - **Customers (User)**: A visitor who has created an account and is logged in.

  <Note>
  These settings are only applicable in the web version of the Gameball customer profile.
  </Note>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Tiers and Leaderboard Visibility" icon="layer-group">
  | **Platform** | **Plan** |
  |--------------|----------|
  | Shopify | Pro & Guru |
  | Salla | Guru |
  | Non-Platform | Add-on (Contact Sales) |

  Choose to show or hide specific tabs:

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/product-docs/customer-widget/tiers-leaderboard-visibility.png" alt="Tiers and leaderboard visibility options" />
  </Frame>

  - **Tiers Card**: Displays the configured tiers and benefits.
  - **Leaderboard Tab**: Shows the leaderboard.
  - **Recent Tiers Benefits**: Displays the benefits of the previous tiers.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="All Games Section" icon="gamepad">
  Control the visibility of the Play & Win section of the widget by deciding to show or hide it. This section includes all the active game campaigns you had set. Note that by hiding this section, game campaigns will act as if they do not exist for your customer, as they will not be able to access them.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/product-docs/customer-widget/all-games-section-visibility.png" alt="All games section visibility" />
  </Frame>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Referrals Visibility" icon="user-plus">
  Choose to show or hide specific sections on **Referrals**:

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/product-docs/customer-widget/referrals-visibility-options.png" alt="Referrals visibility options" />
  </Frame>

  - **Rewards Section**: Choose to show or hide the rewards section on Referrals.
  - **Points in Referrals Page**: Show or hide the points balance inside the referral page.
  - **Referral Image:** Choose to go with the default referral image, upload your own image to match your brand theme, or to leave the referral section on the widget with no image.
  - **Sharing Option**: Choose to display customers' unique link as a full link or rather just a code.

    Example:

    Link: https://xxx.com/account/register?ReferralCode=Vg6945F76CkP1B

    Code: Vg6945F76CkP1B
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Other Features Visibility" icon="sliders">
  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/product-docs/customer-widget/other-features-visibility.png" alt="Other features visibility settings" />
  </Frame>

  - **Notifications Icon**: Optional. Enable or disable the notifications tab as needed.
  - **Gameball Footer**: Choose whether to display the Gameball logo at the bottom of the customer/guest widget.

    <Info>
    The remove footer feature is only available on:

    **Shopify:** **Starter, Pro,** and **Guru** plans.

    **Salla: Pro** and **Guru** plans.

    **Non-Platform:** All.
    </Info>

  - **Gameball FAQ**: Show or hide the FAQ tab, which briefly explains your loyalty program to guests and customers.
  - **Coupons Tab**: Show or hide the Coupons tab, which has the list of all the redeemed coupons from both the Redeem and the Referrals modules on the widget.
  - **Points Card:** Show or hide the current customer's points balance.
  - **Trophy Image:** Show or hide the big trophy image in the widget's home page.

    <Note>
    Hiding of the trophy option will be shown only in case the Tier and Referral cards are set to hidden.
    </Note>

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/product-docs/customer-widget/trophy-image-visibility.png" alt="Trophy image visibility" />
    </Frame>
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Sort Reward Campaigns Badges on Widget

Organize the icons in the **Reward Campaigns** section of the widget by going to the **Reward Campaigns** tab.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/customer-widget/reward-campaigns-sorting.png" alt="Reward campaigns sorting section" />
</Frame>

Select your preferred method for ordering reward campaigns:

- **Creation date**: Display reward campaigns based on the order of their creation.
- **Name**: Alphabetically from A to Z.
- **Manual**: Drag and drop reward campaigns to arrange them as you prefer.

---

## Redemption Options

Manage the display order of your redemption options inside the widget, highlighting key rewards and simplifying navigation for your customers.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/customer-widget/redemption-options-ordering.png" alt="Redemption options ordering" />
</Frame>

Select your preferred method for ordering redemption options:

- **Creation Date**: Displayed chronologically by setup date.
- **Points Required**: Sorted from lowest to highest cost.
- **Manual**: Drag and drop redemption items to reorder them exactly how you like.

---

## Sort Play & Win Options on Widget

When you have multiple game campaigns customized, you will be able to control how they appear in the section as well as their order from the **Play & Win Options** tab under the General widget section in the dashboard.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/customer-widget/play-win-options-sorting.png" alt="Play and win options sorting" />
</Frame>

- Drag and drop each game campaign card.
- Choose the style of each game campaign card. Choose between a square-shaped card or a rectangular-shaped card.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/customer-widget/game-card-layout-options.png" alt="Game card layout options" />
</Frame>

Remember to click **Save** when you are done with ordering.
