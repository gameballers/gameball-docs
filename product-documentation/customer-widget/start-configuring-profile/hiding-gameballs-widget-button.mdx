title: "How Do I Hide Gameball's Widget Button?"

---

If you do not want to choose any of the available Gameball launcher shapes because they do not match your brand theme, or you simply do not have a space for them, you can now hide the widget launcher button, and use Gameball deep links on your platform to open Gameball widget. Read more about [how to use deep links to open Gameball widget](/product-documentation/customer-widget/player-profile-tabs/enable-opening-gameball-widget-through-inline-links-using-deeplinks).

## How to Hide Gameball's Traditional Launcher Button?

On **Gameball Admin Dashboard**, go to **Widget > Launcher: Shape**

Under **Launcher**, choose **Hide** option, then click **Save**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/customer-widget/hide-launcher-shape-option.png" alt="Hide launcher shape option" />
</Frame>

Now, Gameball's launcher button will no longer appear on your platform, so make sure to use our [Deep Links](/product-documentation/customer-widget/player-profile-tabs/enable-opening-gameball-widget-through-inline-links-using-deeplinks) if you still want your customers to access the widget from a different place on your platform.
