---
title: "How Do I Hide Gameball's Widget Button?"
description: "Learn how to hide Gameball widget launcher button from your platform by following some easy steps."
---

If you do not want to choose any of the available Gameball launcher shapes because they do not match your brand theme, or you simply do not have a space for them, you can hide the widget launcher button and use Gameball deep links on your platform to open the Gameball widget. Read more about [how to use deep links to open Gameball widget](/product-documentation/customer-widget/player-profile-tabs/enable-opening-gameball-widget-through-inline-links-using-deeplinks).

---

## How to Hide Gameball's Traditional Launcher Button

<Steps>
<Step title="Navigate to Launcher Shape Settings">
  On the **Gameball Admin Dashboard**, go to **Widget > Launcher: Shape**.
</Step>

<Step title="Choose Hide">
  Under **Launcher**, choose the **Hide** option.
</Step>

<Step title="Save Changes">
  Click **Save** to apply your changes.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/product-docs/customer-widget/hide-launcher-shape-option.png" alt="Hide launcher shape option" />
  </Frame>
</Step>
</Steps>

Now, Gameball's launcher button will no longer appear on your platform. Make sure to use our [Deep Links](/product-documentation/customer-widget/player-profile-tabs/enable-opening-gameball-widget-through-inline-links-using-deeplinks) if you still want your customers to access the widget from a different place on your platform.
