To customize your widget launcher/button, go to **Widget > Launcher: Customize** in the **Gameball Admin Dashboard**.

</Frame>

## Launcher Customization Options

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Icon" icon="image">
  **Uploading an Image to Edit the Launcher Icon is available on:**

  | Platform | Plan |
  |----------|------|
  | Shopify | Pro, Guru |
  | Salla | Pro, Guru |
  | Non-platform clients | Growth, Enterprise |

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/product-docs/customer-widget/launcher-icon-settings.png" alt="Launcher icon settings" />
  </Frame>

  Choose between the following:

  - **From Library:** Choose from Gameball's icons library.
  - **Upload Image:** Upload your own image from your own library to match your theme.

  <Note>
  When choosing **"Upload Image,"** the image size and type must be: 60px x 60px. JPEG or PNG.
  </Note>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Text" icon="font">
  Type down a small text that would appear on the widget button. It could be something simple such as: "Rewards"

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/product-docs/customer-widget/launcher-text-field.png" alt="Launcher text customization field" />
  </Frame>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Visibility & Effect" icon="eye">
  To customize launcher visibility and animation, go to **Widget > Launcher: Shape** in the **Gameball Admin Dashboard**.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/product-docs/customer-widget/launcher-shape-section.png" alt="Launcher shape section" />
  </Frame>

  Under **Launcher**, choose between:

  - **Show:** Ensures the button will be visible to all your players and visitors.
  - **Hide:** Button will not be visible.

  Under **Launcher Effect**, choose between:

  - **Enabled:** Enables a wiggly-like effect to the widget to help customers notice it.
  - **Disabled:** Disregards the animation.

  <Note>
  Hiding the launcher does not deactivate the loyalty program.
  </Note>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Shape" icon="shapes">
  You can control the shape of the profile launcher button. Gameball offers multiple shapes to let you choose from based on your preference or what will be a better match with your website design:

  - **Corner Curve**
  - **Circle**
  - **Center Tab**
  - **Oval With Text:** In this case, you will need to add the text and its color.
  - **Center Tab With Text:** In this case, you will need to add the text and its color.

  <Frame>
    <img src="/images/product-docs/customer-widget/launcher-shape-options.png" alt="Launcher shape options" />
  </Frame>
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Deep Linking" icon="link">
  This will let your customers launch the Gameball customer widget through any button on your website. Learn more about this in [Enable Opening Gameball Widget Through Inline Links Using Deep-links](/product-documentation/customer-widget/player-profile-tabs/enable-opening-gameball-widget-through-inline-links-using-deeplinks).
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
