## How Will Customers View Their Tier Experience?

Customers can easily track their progress and learn about available tiers directly through the **Tiers card** displayed on the **home page of the Gameball widget**. This card shows their **current tier status** at a glance. When customers click on the card, they are taken to a detailed view where they can explore:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
<Card title="Available Tiers" icon="crown">
  View the next tier they can upgrade to and understand what it takes to get there.
</Card>

<Card title="Tier Requirements" icon="flag-checkered">
  See exactly what milestones or spending amounts are needed to reach the next tier.
</Card>

<Card title="Benefits & Perks" icon="gift">
  Explore the exclusive benefits and rewards associated with each tier level.
</Card>

<Card title="Current Tier Status" icon="chart-line">
  Track progress in real time and see how close they are to the next tier.
</Card>
</CardGroup>

This interactive experience ensures customers are always aware of their current tier, how they can progress, and the value they'll receive as they move up.

<Info>
The tiers card is only visible to customers when the tiering program is set to **Active**. While the program is inactive, tiers are hidden from the widget entirely. [Learn more about Program Activation](/product-documentation/programs/vip-tiers/configuring-gameball-vip-tiers#program-activation-and-start-date).
</Info>
