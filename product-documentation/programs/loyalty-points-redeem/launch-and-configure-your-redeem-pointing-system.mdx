Offering customers multiple ways to redeem their points is crucial for enhancing gamification and their loyalty to your brand. With Gameball's versatile redemption system, you can create a seamless and rewarding experience for your customers. By setting up various redemption options, you empower your customers to choose how they want to utilize their points.

In this article, we will walk you through configuring the points redemption system and customizing redemption options to cater to your customers' diverse preferences and needs.

---

## Launching Redeem Loyalty Points

<Steps>
<Step title="Navigate to Redeem Points">
1. Login to your **Gameball Dashboard**.
2. From the left side menu, click on **Programs**.
3. Choose **Redeem Points**, and you will be directed to the Redeem Pointing System configuration page.

<Frame>
  ![Redeem Points Navigation](/images/product-docs/programs/loyalty-points-redeem/redeem-points-navigation.png)
</Frame>
</Step>
</Steps>

---

## Activating the Point-to-Cash Redemption

This allows your customers to convert the points they have in their balance into a cash equivalent.

<Steps>
<Step title="Edit Redemption Options">
Under the redemption options, click on the three dots on the first card then click on edit.
</Step>

<Step title="Set Monetary Value">
Enter the **equivalent monetary value** of one point based on your chosen currency.
</Step>

<Step title="Save and Activate">
Click **Save**.

<Frame>
  ![Point-to-Cash Configuration](/images/product-docs/programs/loyalty-points-redeem/point-to-cash-config.gif)
</Frame>

Make sure the toggle at the upper right of the **Point-to-Cash Redemption** section is set to **Active**.

<Frame>
  ![Point-to-Cash Active Toggle](/images/product-docs/programs/loyalty-points-redeem/point-to-cash-active.png)
</Frame>
</Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
The toggle will remain active unless another redemption rule is added.
</Note>

## Customizing Redemption Options

Gameball offers additional customization options for redemption values. Customers can redeem their points for the following reward types:

### Online Only Rewards

1. **Percentage-Based Discount:** Customers can exchange their points for a coupon that provides a percentage discount on their next purchase.
2. **Fixed Amount Discount:** Customers can redeem their points for a coupon that offers a fixed-value discount on their purchase.
3. **Free Shipping:** Customers can use their points to get free shipping on their order.
4. **Free Product:** Customers can redeem their points to receive specific products at no cost.
5. **Percentage-Based Cashback (Non-Platform Only):** Customers can use a coupon to receive a percentage of their purchase amount back as cashback, credited to their wallet in points or store credit.
6. **Fixed Amount Cashback (Non-Platform Only):** Customers can use a coupon to receive a fixed amount of cashback, credited to their wallet in points or store credit -- regardless of the total purchase amount.
7. **Percentage-Based Fees Discount (Non-Platform Only):** Customers can receive a coupon that applies a percentage discount to transaction fees.

### POS Rewards (Shopify Only)

1. **POS Fixed Amount Coupon:** Redeem points for a specific amount off at point-of-sale.
2. **POS Percentage Coupon:** Redeem points for a percentage discount at point-of-sale.

### Adding a New Redemption Option

<Steps>
<Step title="Add Redemption Option">
On the **Redeem Points** page, click **Add Redemption Option**.
</Step>

<Step title="Configure the Option">
You will be redirected to a new page to add an **Extra Redemption Rule**. Choose the **Redemption Type** and configure the reward based on the type selected.
</Step>

<Step title="Save">
Click **Save**.

<Frame>
  ![Add Redemption Option](/images/product-docs/programs/loyalty-points-redeem/add-redemption-option.gif)
</Frame>
</Step>
</Steps>

Learn more about setting up each type:

- [Set Online Only Redemption Types](/product-documentation/rewards-campaigns/faqs/understanding-rewards-types)
- [Set POS Redemption Types](/product-documentation/guides-strategies-faqs/shopify-guides/spend-points-on-pos)

Once saved, your new rule will appear in the **Custom Redemption Options** grid. Click **More details** to view a full table of all configured redemption rules.

## Settings

### Points Holding Configuration

Set the duration that holds the request until your customer completes the payment process on your mobile app using any other payment method, in case it will be a partial payment using points.

- Choose the **Holding Duration Value** (write a number).
- Select the **Points Holding Time:** Minutes, Hours, or Days (choose the unit of time).

## Insights and Analytics

Use Gameball analytics to track and improve your points redemption strategy.

From the main redemption tab, you can see the following data:

### Total Redemptions

Monitor the total number of redemptions made across all options. This helps assess the popularity and effectiveness of each method.

<Frame>
  ![Total Redemptions](/images/product-docs/programs/loyalty-points-redeem/total-redemptions.png)
</Frame>

### Total Redeemed Points

Track the cumulative number of redeemed points. This metric offers insights into customer engagement and can guide adjustments in your rewards strategy to make data-driven decisions to optimize customer retention and loyalty.

<Frame>
  ![Total Redeemed Points](/images/product-docs/programs/loyalty-points-redeem/total-redeemed-points.png)
</Frame>

### Insights Tab

You can analyze the performance of each **redemption option** directly within the **Redeem Points** section using the new **Insights** tab.

<Frame>
  ![Redeem Insights Tab](/images/product-docs/programs/loyalty-points-redeem/redeem-insights-tab.png)
</Frame>

This tab displays detailed analytics for every redemption option (coupon type). Includes key metrics such as:

| Metric | Description |
|---|---|
| Issued | Number of coupons generated |
| Burned | Coupons redeemed or used |
| Expired | Coupons that expired unused |
| Burn Rate | % of issued coupons that were used |
| Expiry Rate | % of issued coupons that expired unused |
| Total Points | Total points redeemed to generate coupons |

<Tip>
- **Time Range Filter** allows analysis for specific date ranges (e.g., last 30 days).
- **Export Option** lets you download filtered insights in CSV/XLSX format.
- **Default Sorting** is by Total Points (descending), customizable across all columns.
</Tip>
