Offering customers multiple ways to redeem their points is crucial for enhancing gamification and their loyalty to your brand. With Gameball's versatile redemption system, you can create a seamless and rewarding experience for your customers. By setting up various redemption options, you empower your customers to choose how they want to utilize their points.

---

## Navigating to Redeem Points

From the left-side menu, go to **Programs** and click **Redeem Points**. The page opens on the **Configuration** tab, where all your redemption options are listed.

---

## Managing Redemption Options

All configured redemption options appear in the **Redemption Options** section. You can toggle between a **card view** and a **list view** using the icons at the top right of the section.

Each option displays its redemption type, the points required to redeem it, and a toggle to enable or disable it directly.

### Adding a New Redemption Option

<Steps>
<Step title="Click Add Redemption Option">
On the **Redeem Points** page, click **Add Redemption Option**.
</Step>

<Step title="Configure the Option">
You will be taken to the **Custom Redemption Option** page. Fill in the following:

**Rule**
- **Audience** — choose whether this option applies to All Customers or Specific Customers
- **Applies to** — select Entire order or Specific Rule

**Redemption Type**
- Select a redemption type from the dropdown (e.g., Fixed Amount-based Discount, Percentage-based Discount, Free Shipping, Free Product)

**Reward Name**
- Enter the reward name in English (EN) and Arabic (AR) — up to 50 characters each

**Redemption Value**
- **Points Amount** — the number of points required to redeem this option
- **Discount Value** — the value of the discount awarded

**Image**
- Choose **Default** or **Upload Image** to set a custom icon for this redemption option

**Advanced Options** (optional)

| Option | Description |
|--------|-------------|
| **Prefix** | Add a custom prefix to the generated coupon code |
| **Reward expires after specific amount of time** | Set an expiry window for the issued coupon |
| **Minimum purchase amount** | Require a minimum order value for the coupon to apply |
| **Number of reward uses per customer** | Limit how many times a customer can redeem this option (default: 1 time) |
| **Reward combined with other coupons** | Allow this coupon to be stacked with other active coupons |
| **Show "How to redeem" instructions** | Display redemption instructions to customers |
</Step>

<Step title="Create">
Click **Create** to save the redemption option. It will appear in your Redemption Options list.
</Step>
</Steps>

## Available Reward Types

<AccordionGroup>
<Accordion title="Online Only Rewards" icon="globe">

1. **Percentage-Based Discount:** Customers can exchange their points for a coupon that provides a percentage discount on their next purchase.
2. **Fixed Amount Discount:** Customers can redeem their points for a coupon that offers a fixed-value discount on their purchase.
3. **Free Shipping:** Customers can use their points to get free shipping on their order.
4. **Free Product:** Customers can redeem their points to receive specific products at no cost.
5. **Percentage-Based Cashback (Non-Platform Only):** Customers can use a coupon to receive a percentage of their purchase amount back as cashback, credited to their wallet in points or store credit.
6. **Fixed Amount Cashback (Non-Platform Only):** Customers can use a coupon to receive a fixed amount of cashback, credited to their wallet in points or store credit — regardless of the total purchase amount.
7. **Percentage-Based Fees Discount (Non-Platform Only):** Customers can receive a coupon that applies a percentage discount to transaction fees.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="POS Rewards (Shopify Only)" icon="cash-register">

1. **POS Fixed Amount Coupon:** Redeem points for a specific amount off at point-of-sale.
2. **POS Percentage Coupon:** Redeem points for a percentage discount at point-of-sale.
</Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Settings

### Points Holding Duration

Set the duration that holds the redemption request until your customer completes the payment process — for example, when using points as partial payment alongside another payment method.

Click **Edit** next to **Points Holding Duration (Through APIs only)** to configure:

- **Holding Duration Value** — enter a number
- **Points Holding Time** — choose the unit: Minutes, Hours, or Days

## Insights and Analytics

Click the **Insights** tab at the top of the page to analyze the performance of each redemption option.

| Metric | Description |
|---|---|
| Issued | Number of coupons generated |
| Burned | Coupons redeemed or used |
| Expired | Coupons that expired unused |
| Burn Rate | % of issued coupons that were used |
| Expiry Rate | % of issued coupons that expired unused |
| Total Points | Total points redeemed to generate coupons |

<Tip>
- **Time Range Filter** allows analysis for specific date ranges (e.g., last 30 days).
- **Export Option** lets you download filtered insights in CSV/XLSX format.
- **Default Sorting** is by Total Points (descending), customizable across all columns.
</Tip>
