    When you first access Gameball on Shopify, you're guided through a quick setup that launches a fully configured loyalty program in just a few minutes. Based on your inputs, Gameball automatically prepares your program and suggests campaigns aligned with your business goals — no technical setup required.

    ---

    ### Setting Up Your Loyalty Program

    #### Step 1: Meet Sarah

    You'll be greeted by Sarah, who walks you through the setup step by step. Click **Let's get started** to begin.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/product-docs/getting-started/sarah-wizard-intro.png" alt="Sarah wizard introduction screen" />
    </Frame>

    #### Step 2: Answer a Few Questions

    Sarah asks for a few key inputs to tailor the experience to your business:

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Your Industry">
        Sarah uses your industry to set earn rates and tier structure as a starting point. You can adjust everything after setup.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Pricing Tier">
        This tells Sarah the price range of your products so she can calibrate reward values that feel meaningful to your customers.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Growth Goals">
        Sarah uses your goals to decide which features to prioritize — for example, if increasing retention is your main focus, she'll configure the program accordingly.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Widget Style">
        Choose how the loyalty widget looks to your customers. This is what they'll see when they interact with their rewards.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Launch Readiness">
        Confirm you're ready to go. Sarah finalizes your program and hands off to Adam for campaign suggestions.
      </Step>
    </Steps>

    #### Step 3: Your Program Is Ready

    Based on your answers, Gameball automatically:

    - Sets up your loyalty structure
    - Configures your points and rewards logic
    - Prepares your customer-facing widget
    - Aligns everything with your stated business goals

    From here, you can review your setup, make any adjustments, and launch your program. You don't need to handle any technical configuration.

    ---

    ### Revenue Risks & Opportunities

    Once your program is ready, Adam analyzes your Shopify store — order history, customer behavior, and revenue patterns — and surfaces two types of insights on your homepage:

    - **Risk** — A customer segment performing below expected benchmarks on a specific metric, such as AOV, purchase frequency, or basket size. A risk is always tied to a defined segment, not a general signal about overall store performance.
    - **Opportunity** — A segment performing at or above the expected benchmark, or showing clear untapped revenue growth potential based on behavioral signals.

    <Frame>
      ![Adam's homepage showing revenue risk and opportunity cards with revenue impact figures](/images/product-docs/getting-started/adam-risks-opportunities.png)
    </Frame>

    Each card shows a **revenue impact** figure — the dollar amount you stand to gain (opportunities, marked **+**) or lose (risks, marked **−**). This reflects the gap between what the segment generates today and what it could generate if the targeted metric improved, or what you stand to lose if the trend continues (e.g., declining frequency, falling AOV, or customer churn).

    Unactioned risks and opportunities stay at the top of your homepage. Once you launch a campaign against one, it moves to **In Progress Campaigns** — keeping what still needs attention front and center.

    For each risk or opportunity, Adam recommends a campaign with the segment, reward, and budget pre-configured. You can view the full configuration summary, launch directly from the card, or regenerate an alternative.

    ---

    ### Re-generating a Campaign

    If a campaign doesn't feel right for your store, click **Re-generate** on any card.

    When you do this:

    - The risk / opportunity stays the same
    - A new variation is created with adjusted reward amounts and campaign structure

    You can re-generate as many times as you need before launching. This lets you quickly explore different approaches without building a campaign from scratch.

    ---

    ### Launching a Campaign

    When you're happy with a campaign, click **Launch** on its card to open the campaign detail view. Here you can review everything before it goes live:

    - **Audience** — which customers qualify and the estimated ROI
    - **Budget** — the total spend cap for the campaign
    - **Trigger** — the condition a customer must meet to earn the reward (e.g., place an order of 100 AED or more)
    - **Rewards** — the reward type, value, minimum order, expiry, and usage limit

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/product-docs/getting-started/launch-campaign-detail.png" alt="Campaign detail view showing audience, budget, trigger, and rewards before launching" />
    </Frame>

    Click **Launch Campaign** to activate it. The campaign starts tracking customer activity immediately, and customers who meet the conditions will see the reward automatically.

    Once live, the campaign appears under **In Progress Campaigns** while Adam finalizes the setup.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/product-docs/getting-started/adam-in-progress-campaigns.png" alt="Adam's in progress campaigns view" />
    </Frame>

    No further action is needed. You can also rate any suggestion using the thumbs up / thumbs down on each card — your feedback helps improve future recommendations.

    ---

    ### What You Get With This Onboarding

    **Loyalty program setup without expertise** — Gameball generates a complete loyalty program from your Shopify store data, including earning rules, point values, and tier structure. No manual configuration required.

    **Revenue risks and opportunities** — Your homepage surfaces active revenue leakage risks and growth opportunities based on your store's data, with projected revenue impact for each. Unactioned items stay at the top; launched campaigns move to In Progress.

    **AI-recommended campaigns** — For each identified risk or opportunity, Adam recommends a campaign with segment, reward, and budget pre-configured. You can view the full configuration summary, regenerate alternatives, or launch directly.

    **Projected ROI before launch** — Each campaign recommendation includes an estimated revenue impact so you know what to expect before approving.

    **AI-generated campaign visuals** — Generate campaign images using a text prompt directly from the dashboard — no design skills or external tools needed.

    ---

    ### Good to Know

    - You can re-generate campaigns as many times as needed before launching
    - You can edit any campaign after launch from your dashboard
    - Adam's recommendations are based on your store's actual order history, customer behavior, and revenue patterns
