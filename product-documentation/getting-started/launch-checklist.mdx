title: "How Do I Get Started with Gameball?"
description: "A quick walk-through on the essential steps to take before launching your loyalty experience."
---

<Note>
If you are new to Gameball and have just completed the onboarding wizard, you will find a checklist on the dashboard that should guide you through the initial steps to a ready-to-launch program.
</Note>

Follow these essential steps to build and launch your loyalty experience on Gameball.

---

## Launch Checklist

<Steps>
  <Step title="Set Up Customer Access">
    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="Shopify Users">
        Enable account creation on Shopify to give your customers the ability to create accounts and enroll in your Gameball rewards experience. [Learn more](/product-documentation/getting-started/faqs/enable-customer-account-creation-on-shopify)

        You can also make sure that your customers' points and tiers are updated by following the steps in [this article](/product-documentation/understanding-your-customers/your-customers/migrate-your-customers-to-gameball-from-other-loyalty-programs-on-shopify).
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="Non-Shopify Users">
        If you have been using other loyalty programs and want your customers' profiles migrated, follow the steps in [this article](/product-documentation/guides-strategies-faqs/supportive-guides/import-customers-csv) to import customer data to Gameball.
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Up and Activate Gameball Programs">
    Gameball offers programs that help you reach your loyalty goals — whether that's acquisition, activation, engagement, or retention. Configure and activate any of the following:

    <CardGroup cols={2}>
      <Card title="Earn Points & Cashback" icon="coins" href="/product-documentation/programs/loyalty-points-earn/launch-and-configure-your-earn-pointing-system">
        Set up your earning rules and cashback configuration.
      </Card>
      <Card title="Redeem Points" icon="gift" href="/product-documentation/programs/loyalty-points-redeem/launch-and-configure-your-redeem-pointing-system">
        Configure how customers redeem their earned points.
      </Card>
      <Card title="Referrals" icon="users" href="/product-documentation/programs/referral-program/launching-referral-program">
        Launch a referral program to grow your customer base.
      </Card>
      <Card title="Reward Campaigns" icon="bullhorn" href="/product-documentation/rewards-campaigns/reward-campaign-intro">
        Create targeted campaigns to reward specific behaviors.
      </Card>
      <Card title="VIP Tiers" icon="crown" href="/product-documentation/programs/vip-tiers/configuring-gameball-vip-tiers">
        Gamify your experience with tiered loyalty levels.
      </Card>
    </CardGroup>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set Up Redemption Rules">
    After setting up your loyalty programs, configure the right methods and rules for how your customers can redeem their points — whether as discount percentages, coupons, free shipping, or other rewards.

    [Learn more](/product-documentation/programs/loyalty-points-redeem/launch-and-configure-your-redeem-pointing-system)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Customize Your Widget">
    - Create a widget profile and configure general settings such as language, program name, tab visibility, and icons. [Learn more](/product-documentation/customer-widget/introduction-to-customer-widget)

    - Style Gameball's widget to match your store's theme, making it feel like a seamless extension of your brand. [Learn more](/product-documentation/customer-widget/start-configuring-profile/customize-your-gameball-customer-widget-to-match-your-brand)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Activate Your Loyalty Program">
    Once you've configured the essentials, publish the Gameball widget so your customers can enroll in your reward experience. Follow "Stage 1" in the widget visibility guide to go live.

    [Publish Your Widget](/product-documentation/customer-widget/start-configuring-profile/control-gameball-widget-visibility-on-your-platform)
  </Step>
</Steps>
