---
title: "How Do I Enable Customer Account Creation on Shopify?"
description: "A mandatory step to start rewarding your customers using Gameball."
---

In order to start rewarding your customers, you need to enable account creation on Shopify. If your customers are not able to create an account for your Shopify website, they will not be able to enroll in Gameball rewards experience.

## How to Enable Account Creation on Shopify?

To enable account creation, please follow the below steps:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Shopify Settings">
    Sign in to your Shopify admin and click **Settings** in the bottom left corner of the page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Navigate to Checkout Settings">
    Click **Checkout** from the settings options.

    <Frame>
      ![Shopify Settings - Checkout](/images/product-docs/getting-started/faqs/shopify-settings-checkout.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure Customer Accounts">
    Under "Customer Accounts", make sure that you have customer accounts set to either **"optional"** or **"required"**.

    <Frame>
      ![Shopify Customer Accounts Setting](/images/product-docs/getting-started/faqs/shopify-customer-accounts-setting.png)
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Your Changes">
    When you have made the change, click **Save** in order to allow your customers to set up accounts and join Gameball rewards experience.
  </Step>
</Steps>
