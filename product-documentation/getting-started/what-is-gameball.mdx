Gameball is a customer loyalty and gamification platform that helps brands turn one-time buyers into loyal customers who spend 3x more. Instead of building engagement features from scratch, Gameball provides ready-to-deploy infrastructure for loyalty programs, gamification, referrals, and promotions — all manageable from a single dashboard.

Trusted by **3,000+ brands** across **70+ countries**, Gameball processes over **15M API calls daily** and has handled **$7B+ in orders** through our platform.

## Core Modules

Gameball provides six core modules that work together to drive customer engagement and retention:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Loyalty Engine" icon="coins">
    Manage points, cashback, and tier-based programs across your app, website, and stores. Set earning and redemption rules for any customer action, with one unified experience across all channels.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Full Gamification" icon="gamepad">
    Engage users with challenges, missions, streaks, badges, and leaderboards. Our gamification features create habit loops that keep customers coming back — with ready-made UI that requires no development time.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Promotion Engine" icon="tag">
    Run targeted promotions at scale with secure discount and coupon management. Segment customers based on behavior and trigger personalized offers automatically.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Ready Referral" icon="share-nodes">
    Turn your customer base into your best marketing channel. Create referral programs in minutes with shareable links, built-in tracking, and rewards for both sender and receiver.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Automated Journeys" icon="route">
    Build loyalty and marketing workflows visually with our no-code campaign builder. Trigger emails, push notifications, and in-app messages based on real-time customer behavior.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Segments & Analytics" icon="chart-pie">
    Predict customer behavior with actionable insights. Segment users by their actions and characteristics to deliver personalized experiences that drive results.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Platform Support

Gameball integrates seamlessly with your existing stack:

- **E-commerce Platforms** — Direct apps for Shopify and Salla, plus integrations with WooCommerce, Magento, and Salesforce Commerce Cloud.
- **Mobile & Web** — Native SDKs and JavaScript SDK for custom applications, with full API access for headless implementations.
- **POS & Retail** — Connect in-store transactions with online loyalty for a unified omnichannel experience.
- **Marketing Tools** — Pre-built integrations with MoEngage, Braze, Segment, and other CDP/engagement platforms.
