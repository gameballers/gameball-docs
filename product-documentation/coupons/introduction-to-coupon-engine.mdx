## Types of Coupons in Gameball

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Coupons Linked to Gameball Modules" icon="link">
    Coupons can be generated automatically through Gameball features such as:

    - **Reward Campaigns:** Reward customers with discounts for completing campaigns. [Learn more.](/product-documentation/rewards-campaigns/introduction-rewards-campaigns)
    - **Automations:** Automate coupon generation based on triggers like customer anniversaries or purchases. [Learn more.](/product-documentation/automation-campaigns)
    - **Referrals:** Reward advocates with unique coupon codes for successful referrals. [Learn more.](/product-documentation/programs/referrals)
    - **Tiers:** Celebrate customers' loyalty with exclusive tier-based coupons. [Learn more.](/product-documentation/programs/tiers)
    - **Redemption Rules:** Allow customers to redeem points for tailored discounts. [Learn more.](/product-documentation/programs/redemption/launch-configure-redeem-pointing-system)
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Standalone Coupons" icon="ticket">
    Create independent coupons that can be distributed directly to customers without any specific actions required:

    - **Fixed Value Coupons:** Offer a set discount amount (e.g., $10 off).
    - **Percentage-Based Coupons:** Provide a percentage discount (e.g., 15% off).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Customer's List: Coupon Insights Tab

To learn more about each coupon's performance and which customers are engaging with it, pick any created coupon and go to **Customer List**.

<Note>
  This tab is available only under **manually created coupons**.
</Note>

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/product-docs/coupons/coupon-insights.png" alt="Coupon insights" />
</Frame>

You can track coupon usage with detailed order- and customer-level data, helping you measure impact and optimize promotional strategies.

<Steps>
  <Step title="New Insights Tab">
    Available only for **manually created coupons and promotions**. Accessible when you navigate to the **Coupons page** and click on the coupon you want to check.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Redemption Details">
    Each coupon redemption displays:

    | Field | Description |
    |-------|-------------|
    | **Customer ID** | Unique identifier for the customer |
    | **Customer Email** | Email address of the customer |
    | **Discount Value** | The discount applied at redemption |
    | **Date of Use** | When the coupon was redeemed |
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export Option">
    Export coupon insights data to **CSV** for further reporting or analysis.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Permissions">
    Only users with access to the **Coupons section** can view insights.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Coupon Source Visibility in Widget

Customers can now see exactly where each coupon came from, directly inside the Gameball loyalty widget. When a customer views their available coupons in the **Redeem Tab**, each coupon displays its **source** — whether it was issued through a reward campaign, a game, an automation, a VIP tier reward, or a referral.

This transparency helps customers understand how they earned their rewards and encourages them to engage more with your loyalty programs.
