Managing coupons effectively is a vital part of any successful loyalty and marketing strategy. With Gameball's Couponing Engine, you can create, distribute, and track a variety of coupons seamlessly.

In this article, we'll introduce the key features of the Couponing Engine and provide an overview of its capabilities, helping you elevate customer engagement and boost sales.

---

## Types of Coupons in Gameball

### Coupons Linked to Gameball Modules:

Coupons can be generated automatically through Gameball features such as:

- **Reward Campaigns**: Reward customers with discounts for completing campaigns. [Learn more.](/product-documentation/rewards-campaigns/introduction-rewards-campaigns)
- **Automations**: Automate coupon generation based on triggers like customer anniversaries or purchases. [Learn more.](/product-documentation/automation-campaigns)
- **Referrals**: Reward advocates with unique coupon codes for successful referrals. [Learn more](/product-documentation/programs/referrals)
- **Tiers**: Celebrate customers' loyalty by providing exclusive tier-based coupons. [Learn more.](/product-documentation/programs/tiers)
- **Redemption Rules**: Allow customers to redeem points for tailored discounts. [Learn more](/product-documentation/programs/redemption/launch-configure-redeem-pointing-system)

### Standalone Coupons:

Create independent coupons that can be distributed directly to customers without any specific actions required:

- **Fixed Value Coupons**: Offer a set discount amount (e.g., $10 off).
- **Percentage-Based Coupons**: Provide a percentage discount (e.g., 15% off).

## Key Features of Gameball's Couponing Engine

### Comprehensive Management

Gameball simplifies coupon handling with the following features:

- Automatically mark coupons as used, expired, or inactive.
- Validate coupon eligibility and ensure accuracy in coupon application.
- Prevent duplicate coupon names for streamlined tracking.

### Advanced Options

Customize coupons to fit your needs with features such as:

- Adding prefixes or specific characters to coupon codes.
- Setting usage limits (global or per customer).
- Specifying start and expiry dates.
- Activating, deactivating, or duplicating coupons effortlessly.

### Seamless Integration

Coupons integrate seamlessly with all Gameball modules, making it easier to reward, engage, and incentivize your customers.

## Customer's List: Coupon Insights tab

To learn more details about each coupon performance and which of your customers are engaging with it, pick any of you created coupons then go to **Customer List.**

<Note>
This tab is available only under **manually created coupons**.
</Note>

<Frame>
![Coupon insights](/images/product-docs/coupons/coupon-insights.png)
</Frame>

You can now track coupon usage with detailed order- and customer-level data, helping them measure impact and optimize promotional strategies.

### How does it work?

<Steps>
  <Step title="New Insights Tab">
    - Available only for **manually created coupons and promotions**.
    - Accessible when you navigate to the **Coupons page** and click on the coupon you want to check.
  </Step>
  <Step title="View Redemption Details">
    Each coupon redemption will display:
    - **Customer ID**
    - **Customer Email**
    - **Discount Value**
    - **Date of Use**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Export Option">
    Export coupon insights data to **CSV** for further reporting or analysis.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Permissions">
    Only users with access to the **Coupons section** will be able to view insights.
  </Step>
</Steps>
