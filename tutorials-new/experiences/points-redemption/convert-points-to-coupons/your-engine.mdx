# Redeem Through Your Couponing Engine

Integrate Gameball with your platform's existing coupon system (Shopify, WooCommerce, Magento, etc.).

## Overview

This approach allows you to:
- Use your platform's native discounts
- Maintain existing coupon workflows
- Leverage platform-specific features
