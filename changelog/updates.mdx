###  Key Highlights

#### 1. **Action Streak Campaign**

- **Platforms**  
  **Shopify & Salla**: Available on **PRO+ plans**  
  **Other platforms**: Locked by default (available as an **addon**)

- **Channels**  
  Web · Mobile

**Description:**  
The **Action Streak Campaign** rewards customers for completing a specific action multiple times in a row — a powerful driver of repeat engagement and long-term loyalty.

**Use Cases:**
1. **Drive repeat purchases.** Place 3 orders within the month to earn a reward.  
2. **Promote high-value orders.** Make 5 orders over \$100 each to get rewarded.  
3. **Encourage category exploration.** Make 3 orders, each from a different collection (Kids, Women, Men).  
4. **Boost referrals.** Refer 3 friends to earn a premium membership.
