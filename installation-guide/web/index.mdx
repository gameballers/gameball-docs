<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Initialize Gameball Customer Widget" icon="puzzle-piece" href="/installation-guide/web/initialize-widget">
    Get started by integrating the Gameball customer widget into your website or web app. This allows customers to access their profiles, view points, achievements, and interact with personalized features, enhancing their overall experience.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Track Customer Events" icon="chart-line" href="/installation-guide/web/track-events">
    Begin sending your customers' events from your platform to Gameball. By tracking actions like purchases, sign-ups, and other key interactions, you can tailor rewards and communications to engage your customers more effectively.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Track Orders & Cashback Reward" icon="shopping-cart" href="/installation-guide/web/track-orders">
    Monitor your customers' orders to provide cashback rewards and recognize achievements. This helps in encouraging repeat purchases and building customer loyalty by rewarding them for their activities on your platform.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Integrate Redemption" icon="gift" href="/installation-guide/web/integrate-redemption">
    Enable your customers to redeem their earned points directly through your platform. This seamless integration makes it easy for customers to use their rewards, promoting continued engagement and satisfaction.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Track Referrals" icon="users" href="/installation-guide/web/track-referrals">
    Reward your customers for referring new users and help grow your business organically. By tracking referral activities, you can incentivize customers to spread the word, expanding your user base through word-of-mouth marketing.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Go-Live Checklist" icon="check-circle" href="/installation-guide/web/go-live-checklist">
    Use this comprehensive checklist to ensure a smooth transition when taking your integration live. It helps you verify all components are working correctly, providing a flawless experience for your customers from day one.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
