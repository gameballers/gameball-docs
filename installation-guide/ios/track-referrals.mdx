Reward your customers for referring new users and help grow your business organically. By tracking referral activities, you can incentivize customers to spread the word, expanding your user base through word-of-mouth marketing.

## How Referral System Works

<Steps>
<Step title="Generate Referral Link">
  Create a unique referral link for each customer through the Gameball system
</Step>

<Step title="Share Referral Link">
  Customer shares their referral link with friends, family, or on social media
</Step>

<Step title="Track Referral Clicks">
  Gameball tracks when someone clicks on a referral link
</Step>

<Step title="Monitor Sign-ups">
  Track when referred users create accounts or make purchases
</Step>

<Step title="Award Rewards">
  Both the referrer and referee receive rewards based on your program configuration
</Step>
</Steps>
